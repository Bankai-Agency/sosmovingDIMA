## What is a good way to meet your neighbors after moving to NYC from California?  

When it comes to moving, most people focus all their attention on the actual process of moving and working with [movers California to New York](https://sosmovingla.net/services/interstate-move/california-to-new-york/), rather than the aftermath of the move. Of course, there is not wrong with that, but you also need to have some type of plan. It doesn’t have to be elaborate as a moving plan but you should have *some key things figured out*. This includes the route to work, important government institutions, and meeting new neighbors.

If you are not an extrovert, it’s **not easy to approach an unfamiliar person** and start conversations with them. Also, add stress and exhaustion from moving, you don’t feel like doing anything much less meeting new people. However, you know [how much neighbors are important](https://www.eplace.com.au/lifestyle/why-knowing-your-neighbours-is-more-important-than-you-think) and for that reason, you have a few ways to meet them.

- Say hello  - Involve in neighborhoods activities  - Be more outside  - Go introduce yourself  - Ask or offer help

## Saying hello can be a good start  when you want to meet your neighbors after moving to NYC from CA

Politeness can melt even the coldest heart in the world. This doesn’t mean your new neighbors will be cold people but if they are, *being polite is a good strategy*. When meeting new people, the first impression is very important. For this reason, you should make sure that your [appliance movers Los Angeles](https://sosmovingla.net/services/appliance-move/) don’t block the entrance to your neighbor’s driveway. This way, they will think about how nice and polite you are and they can even approach you first.

If you need to [unpack in a day after moving from CA to NYC](https://sosmovingla.net/how-to-unpack-in-a-day-after-moving-from-ca-to-nyc/), you can ask for help from your new neighbors. Of course, you shouldn’t task them with unpacking the whole household as that’s a big no. Maybe ask them for help when unpacking outdoor items and at the same time you can **find out about the lifestyle and community** in the new neighborhood.

## How to involve in neighborhood activities?  

The best way to meet your neighbors after moving to NYC from CA nowadays is *through social media*. Almost all neighborhoods have groups on some platform where neighbors share the newest happenings, problems, and ideas. You can first introduce yourself to next-door neighbors and ask them about the best way to meet other people. They can introduce you to other neighbors, add to the group chat on social media, and generally show you the workings of the neighborhood.
