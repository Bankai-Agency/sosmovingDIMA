## Tips for packing large and bulky items

After you disassemble your large and bulky items, it is time to pack them alone or with [movers California to New York](https://sosmovingla.net/services/interstate-move/california-to-new-york/). You should put all the small bolts in other parts into zip bags. Make sure to label those bags so you will not lose any bolts when you need to reassemble your items again. Then, for larger parts that cannot fit into bags, *you need to use bubble wrapping*. Make sure to secure bubble wrapping with either tape or moving straps. You should put those pieces in two moving boxes. Again, make sure to label moving boxes. If you cannot disassemble your large items, then you will have to move them in one piece. Still, you will have to protect him from any potential damage. You will do this by covering them with moving blankets. Make sure to secure the blanket with moving straps.

## Transporting your items

After you’re done with packing large and bulky items, it is time to transport them to the moving truck. As mentioned before, you need to **use a dolly for this purpose**. It is going to be much easier if you use a dolly instead of carrying large and bulky items in your hands. This is the part where usually your movers will step in and do it for you. Your main job is to properly pack and secure all the items so they will not sustain any damage during the transport. If you happen to do this part on your own as well, make sure to find the right place for your items in the moving truck. This means they won’t be able to move during transport.
