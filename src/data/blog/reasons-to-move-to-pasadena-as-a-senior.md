## Move to Pasadena as a senior because of the weather

Most people after they reach the age of 70 react differently to the weather. It is better for seniors to spend their time in an area where the weather is warm. This is the first reason why you should move to Pasadena after your retirement. This city has **a Mediterranean climate**. This means that it has long hot summers that last well until late October and somewhat cold winters. Usually, June, July, and August are the hottest with **90 degrees** on average while December, January, and February are the coldest with **40 degrees on average**. This means that you can spend most of your time outside, taking a long walk, socializing with other people, and enjoying your life. ## Move to Pasadena as a senior because of the living costs

If you live in a huge city, it can be quite expensive. Unfortunately, you are going to receive less money when you retire. Of course, this depends on your job position before your retirement. However, usually, **it is less than your salary**. You need to find a city with acceptable living costs so you can afford your life there. Pasadena is cheaper than any large city in its vicinity. It is a little bit more expensive than other smaller cities. Still, it is pretty affordable. For example, a median family income is around** $76,000** while a median home price would be around **$470,000**. Since you will probably live only with your partner, you can save money by [buying a smaller house](https://sosmovingla.net/tips-for-living-in-smaller-spaces/) rather than a huge family one. In addition to this, you can also see if you can buy a house with a small garden or a backyard.

## Culture & Contemporary Life

Pasadena is everything but a boring city. You will find all sorts of entertainment options geared toward all age groups. First of all, you should start your every new year with **the Tournament of Roses Parade**. It is one of the most popular parades in the country filled with roses and marching bands. Then, you should visit the downtown as it is the most interesting place in the city. There are many restaurants, shops, yoga places, cultural centers, and so on in this area. In addition to this, **you can also find senior groups** where you can join, meet new people, find a new hobby, and something similar. It is important to find a group of people to spend time with. This is especially important when you a senior. You should still be active despite your age and engage in activities that you can handle.

## Move to Pasadena as a senior because of the food and parks

Another important thing to consider when moving to a new city would be the food. Luckily, Pasadena has one of the best food scenes in the entire state. You can find everything from *Japanese food, Indian food, Mexican food, and so on*. After a good meal, it is time to be active. There are many parks around the city where you can walk around, breathe the fresh air, and spend time with other people. This is also perfect **if you have a pet**. As you might know, it is extremely [beneficial to have a pet](https://www.cdc.gov/healthypets/health-benefits/index.html), especially if you live alone. For this reason, after your move, you should consider adopting a cat or a dog. If you adopt a dog, then you need to take them for a walk every single day. This is also a perfect opportunity to leave **your house every day and be physically active**.
