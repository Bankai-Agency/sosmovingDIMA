## What is the best strategy for packing toys?

Toys are a very diverse category that contains everything from small figurines, plushy toys, to huge and complex toys. For this reason, you should *group them by similarity* as this will make your packing much easier than packing randomly. For this reason, you should first wash plushy toys and then pack them in a cardboard box in a larger size. As these toys are usually huge in size but light in weight, large boxes are just perfect for them.
