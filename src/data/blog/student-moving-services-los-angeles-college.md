**Volume challenges:**Thousands moving together. Same buildings, same days. Elevator bottlenecks everywhere. Parking impossible. Lines for everything. Stress levels maximum.

**Budget constraints:**Students broke perpetually. Parents already paying tuition. Every dollar matters. Shared cost splits. Financial aid delays. Work-study limitations.

**Unique logistics:**Dorm room tiny spaces. Shared room coordination. Building restrictions numerous. International student needs. Storage over breaks. Cross-country coordination.

## Dorm Room Realities

**What fits:**Twin XL bed (provided). Desk and chair (provided). Mini-fridge (2.5-4 cubic feet). Microwave (if allowed). 3-4 boxes belongings. Closet stuff minimal.

**What doesn't:**Full-size anything. Extra furniture usually. Large TVs increasingly. Gaming chairs. Exercise equipment. Excessive decorations.

**Common mistakes:**Bringing entire bedroom. Multiple season wardrobes. Full kitchen supplies. Library of books. Furniture from home. Too much everything.
