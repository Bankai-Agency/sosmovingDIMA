Friday 6pm: employees leave. Monday 8am: they return to a completely different address with everything functioning. After relocating hundreds of LA businesses over weekends, here's how professional commercial moving minimizes downtime to zero.

## Why Weekends Are Essential

**Downtime cost calculation:**

- 50-employee company- $40K daily revenue average- Each hour closed = $5,000 lost- Tuesday move = $40,000 minimum loss

**Weekend move:**

- Zero revenue loss- No employee disruption- Client services maintained- Momentum preserved

**The math is obvious. Execution is complex.**

## The 53-Hour Window

**Friday 6pm - Monday 7am breakdown:**

**Friday evening (6pm-midnight):**

- IT shutdown procedures- Final backup complete- Sensitive documents secured- Initial loading begins

**Saturday (all day):**

- Main moving operations- Heavy furniture transport- Bulk relocation

**Sunday (all day):**

- IT setup priority- Testing systems- Final placement- Emergency fixes

**Monday morning buffer:**

- Final adjustments- Issue resolution- Staff orientation ready

**Two weeks before:**

- Building management meetings- Elevator reservations- Security access arranged- IT migration plan- Employee communication- Parking permits secured- Floor plans finalized

**One week before:**

- Color coding system implemented- Packing supplies distributed- Priority items identified- Backup plans confirmed

## IT Infrastructure Priority

**The nightmare scenario:**Furniture perfect, computers dead, business destroyed

**IT moving sequence:**

- Complete backup (Wednesday)- Document configurations- Proper shutdown (Friday)- Climate-controlled transport- Priority reinstallation (Saturday night)- Testing (Sunday morning)- Full functionality (Sunday night)- Verification (Monday 6am)

**Never trust "it'll just work"**
