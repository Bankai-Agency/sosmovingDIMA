## Review Analysis

**Where to check:**

- Google Reviews (most reliable)- Yelp (but manipulated)- BBB (complaints matter)- Facebook (real photos)- NextDoor (neighbor experiences)

**Red flags in reviews:**

- How they handle problems- Specific details mentioned- Photos included- Response quality- Recent patterns
