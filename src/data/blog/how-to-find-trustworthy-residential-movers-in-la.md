## Always ask about the license

You might be wondering if movers need a license to be professional movers. The answer is that *they definitely need to have a license*. However, the professional mover’s license depends on what state they work in and if they do interstate move. In the case of interstate movers, they have to register with [the U.S. Department of Transportation](https://www.transportation.gov/). After registration, they receive a unique USDOT number. This number means that movers are operating interstate and they have met all the criteria. The local movers are given the license by the state they operate in.

The trustworthy movers should have** vast experience in relocating**. You should rather hire experienced movers than [risk it with amateur CA movers](https://sosmovingla.net/the-risks-of-hiring-amateur-ca-movers/). It’s okay to give someone a chance, but do you really want to risk your items getting damaged? Well, the logical answer is no.
