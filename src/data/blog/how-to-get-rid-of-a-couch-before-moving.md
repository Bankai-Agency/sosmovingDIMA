That 10-year-old couch seemed worth moving until you calculated costs: $100 to move it, but replacement costs $400 at IKEA. Now you're stuck with 200 pounds of furniture nobody wants, pickup services quoting $150, and moving day approaching fast. Here's every legal way to get rid of your couch in Los Angeles, ranked from free to expensive, fastest to slowest.

## Free Couch Removal Options

**LA Sanitation Bulk Pickup (FREE, 1-2 weeks):**Best option for city residents. Schedule at MyLA311.lacity.org or call 1-800-773-2489. Place couch at curb night before. They take up to 4 items. Cannot be blocked by cars.

Wait times:

- Regular season: 7-10 days- Peak moving season: 2-3 weeks- After holidays: 3-4 weeks

**"Free" Section Success (FREE, 0-3 days):**

**Craigslist Free:**Post with photos. Title: "FREE COUCH - FIRST COME - CURB ALERT"Gone in: 2-48 hours usually

**Facebook Marketplace:**List as free. Reaches neighbors quickly.Success rate: 70% within 3 days

**Nextdoor:**"Free couch, must go today!"Best for: Same neighborhood pickup

**Key to success:** Good photos, honest description, "first come first served," location visible.

## Donation Options (Sometimes Free Pickup)

**Who actually takes couches:**

**Habitat for Humanity ReStore**

- Condition: Excellent only- Pickup: Free for multiple items- Call: (323) 989-4092- Reality: Reject 80% of couches

**Out of the Closet**

- Condition: Good, no stains- Pickup: Sometimes free- Multiple locations in LA- Benefit: AIDS Healthcare Foundation

**Salvation Army**

- Pickup: Hit or miss- Call: (800) SA-TRUCK- Scheduling: 2-3 weeks out- Often cancel last minute

**Reality check:** Most charities reject used couches. Bed bugs fear, stains, wear. Don't count on donation.

## Sell for Removal Price

**Price to move strategy:**Your couch worth $500? List for $50. Buyer handles removal. You avoid disposal costs.

**Where to list:**

- Facebook Marketplace: Fastest- OfferUp: Local buyers- Craigslist: Still works- Nextdoor: Neighbors

**Magic words:** "Priced to move today!" "Must go by [date]" "You haul"

Success rate: 60% if priced right

## Paid Removal Services

**Junk haulers:**

- 1-800-GOT-JUNK: $129-189 (same day)- College Hunks: $100-150- LoadUp: $79-129- TaskRabbit: $50-100

**What's included:**

- They lift/carry- Same or next day- Stairs no problem- Multiple items possible

**Hidden costs:** Minimum charges apply. One couch costs same as three.

## The Apartment Dumpster Dilemma

**Legal?** Usually no.**Risk:** $500 fine, angry management**Reality:** People do it anyway

**If you must:**

- Break down first- Late night/early morning- Not all at once- Mix with regular trash

**Better option:** Ask management about bulk disposal days

## DIY Disposal Routes

**Rent truck, drive to landfill:**

- U-Haul: $29.95 + mileage- Landfill fee: $35-50- Gas: $20- Your time: 3 hours- Total: $100+

**Locations accepting couches:**

- Calabasas Landfill- Scholl Canyon Landfill- Southeast Resource Recovery

Must show LA residency.

## Breaking Down for Regular Trash

**Tools needed:**

- Reciprocating saw: $40- Box cutter- Screwdriver- 2 hours patience

**Process:**

- Remove cushions (trash bag)- Cut fabric (trash bag)- Disassemble frame (regular trash)- Cut foam (multiple bags)- Spread over 2-3 weeks

Legal but exhausting.

## Special Circumstances

**Bed bugs/infested:**

- MUST wrap completely in plastic- Mark "BED BUGS" clearly- Special pickup required- Costs $200-400- Illegal to donate/give away

**Leather couches:**

- Harder to donate (smell issues)- Recycling centers sometimes interested- Upholstery shops might want leather

**Sleeper sofas:**

- Extra heavy (contains bed frame)- Most expensive to remove- Scrap metal value inside

## Timing Your Disposal

**Start early:**

- 3 weeks before: Try selling- 2 weeks before: Schedule free pickup- 1 week before: List as free- 3 days before: Call junk haulers- Day before: Panic options

Don't wait until moving day.

## Illegal Disposal Penalties

**Street dumping:** $500-1,500 fine**Alley dumping:** $500 fine + cleanup costs**Other's dumpster:** Trespassing charges**Vacant lot:** Criminal prosecution possible

LA has cameras everywhere. You will get caught.

## Couch Conditions Nobody Wants

**Automatic rejections:**

- Pet damage/smell- Visible stains- Torn fabric- Broken frame- Cigarette burns- Missing cushions- Bed bugs ever

If yours has these, go straight to paid removal.

## The "Moving Sale" Strategy

**Three days before moving:**

- Everything must go sale- Couch priced at $25- "Free if you take today"- Usually works

People buy things at moving sales they'd never buy otherwise.

Usually cheaper to dispose and buy new.
