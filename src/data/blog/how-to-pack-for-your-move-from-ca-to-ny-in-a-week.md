The time has come for you to move from CA to NYC. However, you have very limited time to prepare and pack your items. Usually, it takes at least a month or two to organize your move, find the [best moving companies Los Angeles](https://sosmovingla.net/), and pack everything. You can achieve all this even in a short time frame. If you want to succeed in such a short time, **you should focus mostly on packing**. Packing is usually the most tiring and time-consuming part of the move. Additionally, you can’t achieve this task all by yourself. For this reason, you should either hire professional movers or get help from friends and family. If you choose the second option, here are a few tips on how to pack for your move from CA to NY in a week.

## Where to start when you need to pack for your move from CA to NY in a week?

The first step in every move is to set *your moving date*. Even if your move has to happen in a week, there are still differences between certain days. Weekends are usually very busy for [Los Angeles interstate movers](https://sosmovingla.net/services/interstate-move/) and you will have a hard time finding free movers. Even if you find one, they will probably charge more than on other days. For this reason, you should choose weekdays over weekends. Also, if you can, you should choose early mornings over middays or evenings. The weather is usually very warm in California and if you are going to move in the early morning, you will avoid intense heat and traffic jams.

## Pack for your move from CA to NY in a week after decluttering your home

Decluttering your home is an important but often forgotten step in moving. If you have a big home with many belongings, you will hardly succeed to pack all of them in a week. For this reason, *decluttering is a must*. A small number of belongings means less time for packing but also it will require a smaller amount of moving supplies. You will be saving time and money just by doing a simple declutter. This means decluttering items that are broken, damaged, unused for a long time, and outgrown items. You can donate baby clothes and toys in good condition to charities that like Goodwill, The Salvation Army, Baby2Baby, and many others. Always choose to donate to charities instead of throwing them into the trash for items that are in good condition and clean.

## Pack for your move from CA to NY in a week without movers

If you choose to pack your household without [movers California to New York](https://sosmovingla.net/services/interstate-move/california-to-new-york/), you will need big help. Ask your family, friends, colleagues, and everyone you know for help. After you found who will help you, you should first **pack an essential bag**. As New York has colder weather than California, you should pack some warmer clothes, bathroom utilities, beddings, medication, food, documents, etc. When it comes to packing the rest of your belongings, you should make a packing plan. Start packing one room and don’t start another room until you completely finish first. This way, you won’t get lost in packing and lose all hope and will to finish it. For this reason, it very useful to have a packing plan to follow.

## Packing is not simply putting items into the moving boxes

Some items require more attention than others. You can’t put fragile items into moving boxes and call it packing. Most of the items, especially fragile ones, need to be wrapped first with *bubble wrapping and packing paper*. Only then you can put these items into moving boxes. Also, use different types of cushioning in moving boxes to prevent damage to your items during transport. Lastly, you should write Fragile on every box that contains this type of belongings.
