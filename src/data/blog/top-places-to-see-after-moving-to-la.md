## Natural History Museum of Los Angeles County is a very interesting place to see after moving to LA

The Natural History Museum has many galleries and a big permanent collection which covers a wide range of topics. It is probably the most famous for its collection of dinosaurs. Moreover, it *has three full skeletons of Tyrannosaurus rex.* It also has Triceratops and Stegosaurus amongst many others. When talking about places to see after moving to LA the [Natural History Museum](https://nhm.org/) is definitely one of the top locations to visit.
