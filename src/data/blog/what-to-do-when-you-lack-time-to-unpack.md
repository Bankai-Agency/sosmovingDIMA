Most people think moving is over once they reach the new house. After several weeks, sometimes even months, of organizing relocation, finding [Los Angeles movers](https://sosmovingla.net/los-angeles-movers/), and packing everything properly, no one thinks about the after-move period. This is understandable since the pre-moving period is quite stressful. However, **you need to think about how to unpack your items once you move into your new house.** This can be also as stressful as packing your items. Since you might be in a hurry to get your life back on track, you might not have enough time to unpack everything the way you want it. What to do when you lack time to unpack? Here are a couple of solutions.

## Make a plan even if you lack time to unpack 

You should know by now that is really important to make a plan when you are moving. Your pre-moving plan should have included hiring [long distance movers Los Angeles](https://sosmovingla.net/services/long-distance-movers/), getting packing supplies, decluttering your house, packing everything, finishing all the bureaucracy, and so on. *Now that you finally move into your new house, it is time to make a new plan.* This time it is for unpacking your items. First of all, you should not stress about unpacking your items. This time, you do not have a deadline since a moving company is not going to come to pick up your items. You can take it slow and easy. Then,* take a look at your boxes.* See how many you have and how much time approximately you need for each box to unpack. Then, you can make a plan.

## Try giving yourself a deadline

As mentioned, you don’t have to give yourself a deadline. However, it can be beneficial for you if you have a certain deadline to meet. If you’re one of those people who work better if they have a certain date until they have to finish one task, then feel free to give yourself one. Movers Palmdale CA have already delivered your items, so you only need to unpack them. How to **give yourself a deadline**? For example, most people like to organize a housewarming party. In order to do this, your house should be somewhat clean and set up. It would not be comfortable for your guests to run into boxes while you are showing them your new house. For this reason, tell yourself that you should unpack all the boxes until your party. If you are not organizing any party, then just pick a date that you like.

## Start easy when you lack time to unpack

The best way to go about unpacking items would be to start easy. It can be quite stressful if you see a lot of boxes waiting for you to unpack them. You should avoid additional stress because it’s not going to be good for your health. For this reason,** you can see if **[**packing services Los Angeles**](https://sosmovingla.net/services/packing-services/)** also have unpacking services.** This is a perfect solution for someone who doesn’t have time, energy, or will do unpack their boxes. However, this is going to be a little bit more expensive since movers will come and do everything instead of you. If this is not an option for you, then start easy. If you feel exhausted after moving, do not go to unpack your kitchen or closet. Leave these rooms for later. The first room you should unpack would be your *bedroom and bathroom*.

## Do your essential box first 

Since you do not have enough time to unpack everything, you should start with your essential box. The same applies if you are moving your office with [office movers Los Angeles](https://sosmovingla.net/services/commercial-movers/). You won’t be able to work in your office unless you have all the necessary items set up. In your office, these items would be computers and all the important documents. On the other hand, all the essential items in your house might be a little bit different. First of all, **you need to make your bed** **so you will have a place to sleep.** then, you will need to set up your bathroom because it is also necessary to use it every single day. Then, you will need to plug in all the household appliances in your kitchen so you can use them. Everything else can wait until you have more time.

## Ask for help when you lack time to unpack 

If your family members or friends are now living close to you, you can ask them to help you. This is probably the most efficient way to unpack your items. Since you are closed to your family members and friends, you will be comfortable enough with having them touching your items. For this reason, see if your family members or friends are free to help you out. *This is also a perfect opportunity to catch up and talk about your move and life.* You’re combining something practical with enjoyable. You get to spend some quality time with either your family members or with your friends while unpacking your house. In addition to this, if you have one or maybe more people to help you out, you will finish much faster than anticipated. This is also a good way to [relieve stress](https://www.verywellmind.com/tips-to-reduce-stress-3145195) after your relocation.

## More ways to organize your time 

As you can see, it is not the end of the world if you lack time to unpack your boxes. All you need to do is **take your time and do it slowly**. For this reason, here are even more suggestions when you need to unpack your items.

- You should play music while you’re unpacking. It would be smart to avoid watching TV or binge-watching your favorite show since you might not be as efficient as you should be. - Put items in your way so you will have no other option but to unpack them. - Do not forget to reward yourself after a job well done. If you promise yourself that you can do something that you really wanted for a long time, you will be more motivated to finish your unpacking.
