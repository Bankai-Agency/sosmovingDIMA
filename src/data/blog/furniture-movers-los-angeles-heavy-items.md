**Weight misconceptions:**Modern sectional sofa: 250-400 pounds. Solid wood dresser: 200-350 pounds. King-size bed frame: 150-250 pounds. Armoire/wardrobe: 300-500 pounds. Dining table (wood): 200-400 pounds. China cabinet: 300-450 pounds.
