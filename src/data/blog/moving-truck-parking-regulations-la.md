Your 26-foot rental truck doesn't fit in normal parking, blocks the bike lane, and now has three tickets totaling $279 plus a boot on the wheel. Welcome to moving truck parking in Los Angeles, where every street has different rules, commercial vehicle restrictions apply everywhere, and parking enforcement shows no mercy. Here's how to legally park a moving truck in LA without funding the city budget.

## Commercial Vehicle Restrictions

**The brutal truth:**Moving trucks are commercial vehicles. Different rules apply.

**Prohibited areas:**

- Residential streets (without permit)- 2am-6am anywhere residential- Metered spaces over 22 feet- Any street under 1-ton restriction- Posted "No Commercial Vehicles"

**Time limits:**

- Commercial loading: 30 minutes- Yellow zones: 5-30 minutes varies- White zones: 3 minutes (passenger only)- Green zones: Can't use at all

**One hour in wrong spot = $93 minimum**

## The Permit Solution

**Temporary no-parking permits:**Your only real option for residential streets

**Requirements:**

- Apply 72 hours before- Post signs properly- $40 per sign- 2-4 signs typically needed

**Process:**

- Go to DOT website- Select location- Pay fee- Print signs- Post 72 hours early- Hope people respect them

**Success rate: 60% on good days**

## Size Matters Desperately

**Truck sizes vs LA streets:**

**10-foot truck:**

- Fits most spaces- Still commercial vehicle- Easier maneuvering

**15-foot truck:**

- Borderline impossible- Takes 1.5 spaces

**20-foot truck:**

- Requires 2+ spaces- Height clearance issues

**26-foot truck:**

- Urban nightmare- Professional territory- Don't attempt downtown

**Bigger truck = exponentially harder parking**

## Neighborhood-Specific Rules

**Downtown:**

- No street parking period- Loading zones only- $50/hour parking garages (if fit)- Constant enforcement

**Beverly Hills:**

- 2-hour limit everywhere- Overnight prohibition- Aggressive ticketing- $100+ fines

**Santa Monica:**

- Permit required always- Height restrictions (trees)- Beach area impossible

**Hollywood:**

- Film shoot conflicts- Tourist zone restrictions- Hills impossible

**Valley:**

- More lenient generally- Still need permits- Wide streets helpful

**Red zone:**$93 minimum

## Loading Zone Navigation

**Yellow commercial zones:**

- 30 minutes maximum usually- Active loading required- Can't leave unattended- Competition fierce

**Finding zones:**

- LA DOT map online- Usually near businesses- Morning competition highest- Rare in residential

**White zones (passenger only):**Don't even try. $93 instantly.

## Time Restrictions Maze

**Street cleaning:**

- Weekly everywhere- Different days each side- Posted signs small- No exceptions ever- Tow plus ticket

**Rush hour restrictions:**

- 7-9am and 4-7pm typical- Major streets only- Becomes tow-away- Check every sign

**Overnight parking:**

- Many streets prohibit 2-6am- Commercial vehicles especially- Residents exempt (with permit)

Read every sign. Then read again.
