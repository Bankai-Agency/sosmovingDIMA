## How to find a suitable moving company?

It is not easy to find a suitable moving company. Unfortunately, there are many frauds working in the moving industry. They will do anything but help you or your family members to move safely to your new house. For this reason, when you are searching for [moving companies Pasadena](https://sosmovingla.net/los-angeles-movers/pasadena-movers/), you should do research. First of all, when you’re searching for a moving company, make sure to be as specific as possible. Then, **pick three to five companies that you like at the first glance**. You should read moving reviews to see what previous customers have to say about their services. Then, when you contact moving companies, make sure to ask for their licenses. Every reliable moving company should have a license issued by some type of government institution. Lastly, ask your seniors what they think about moving companies that you choose.

## Collecting packing supplies and decluttering the house

The best way to save money would be to collect packing supplies on your own and to declutter the house before relocation. First of all, if you want to **collect moving supplies by yourself**, you have several choices. You can either visit your local supermarkets, stores, offices, and similar places to get cardboard boxes. Make sure these boxes are in good condition. You can also find shredded paper, bubble wrapping, tape, and similar items at these places. The only thing that you should buy would be plastic boxes, moving blankets, packing peanuts, and so on. In addition to this, it is extremely necessary to declutter the house before relocation. You should help your seniors to decide what they want to do with their items. ## Help elderly people move to California and assist with packing

The last thing you should help your seniors when moving would be to pack their items. Of course, this doesn’t mean you have to do everything alone. Rather, you should *work together and pack their household items*. It would be better to make a packing plan so you can schedule the days and hours when you can dedicate your time to packing. Since you need to consider the age and physical condition of your elderly, you should not pack items for more than a couple of hours per day. In addition to this, you should be the one packing heavy and large household appliances, furniture, and so on. Your seniors can be responsible for packing smaller and lighter items such as clothes, dishes, and similar. If you are the one doing the packing, make sure you pack everything properly. In any case, get proper moving insurance.

You should also help elderly people moved to California on** their moving day as well**. Be there on a moving day so you can welcome movers and assist if necessary. Your seniors can offer refreshments and maybe food if they want to. After everything is loaded in the moving truck, together with your seniors, you should take a walk around the house to see if everything was packed and moved to the moving truck. Lastly, you should help with unpacking their item as well once they move into their new house.
