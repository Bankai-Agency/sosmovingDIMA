## How hiring movers can help you be more environmentally-friendly in CA    

The first rule of having an eco-friendly move is to produce less waste whenever possible. You can achieve this by having fewer items to move. For this reason, you will have to *declutter your home before you start packing*. All items that you don’t use and won’t need in your new home, you should either sell, donate, or thrown away. In this particular case, you should try to donate or sell most of your items. Another good option is to [recycle items](https://www.rubicon.com/blog/what-can-be-recycled/) that you can in your area.

In addition to this, the biggest part of the move and waste is made of** packing supplies**. If you hire [moving companies Pasadena](https://sosmovingla.net/pasadena-movers/), they can tell you how many moving boxes and other supplies you will need. This way, you won’t buy more than you need, so you won’t be creating unnecessary waste already at the beginning.
