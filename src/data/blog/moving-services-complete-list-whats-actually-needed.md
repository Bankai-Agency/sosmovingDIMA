Moving companies offer 20+ services ranging from essential to ridiculous. Full packing saves time but costs $2,000. Custom crating protects valuables but runs $500 per item. Unpacking service sounds convenient until you realize paying $35/hour to put your underwear in drawers. Here's the complete moving services breakdown—what's worth buying, what's overpriced, and what you should skip entirely.

## Essential Services (Buy These)

**Basic moving crew:**

- Loading/unloading- Transportation- Basic equipment- **Cost:** Included in hourly rate- **Value:** Mandatory foundation

**Protective materials:**

- Moving blankets- Shrink wrap- Floor protection- **Cost:** $50-100 or included- **Worth it:** Prevents damage exceeding cost

**Disassembly/reassembly:**

- Bed frames- Table legs- Basic furniture- **Cost:** $15-25 per piece- **Value:** Saves time, prevents damage

**These three services form moving essentials**

## High-Value Services

**Professional packing (fragile items only):**

- Kitchen dishes/glassware- Artwork- Electronics- **Cost:** $35-45/hour per packer- **Value:** Expertise prevents damage

**Specialty item handling:**

- Pianos- Gun safes- Hot tubs- **Cost:** $150-500 per item- **Necessity:** Required for safety/insurance

**Wardrobe boxes:**

- Hanging clothes transfer- Wrinkle prevention- **Cost:** $12-15 each- **Value:** Time savings significant

## Questionable Services

**Full-house packing:**

- Everything packed professionally- **Cost:** $1,500-3,000- **Value:** Convenient but expensive- **DIY savings:** $1,000+

**Unpacking service:**

- Items removed from boxes- **Cost:** $25-35/hour- **Reality:** You reorganize anyway- **Skip unless:** Physically unable

**Cleaning service:**

- Old/new home cleaning- **Cost:** $200-500- **Alternatives:** Hire cleaners directly (cheaper)- **Skip:** Unless bundled discount significant

## Overpriced Add-Ons

**Moving supplies markup:**

- Boxes: 300% markup typical- Tape: 400% markup- Bubble wrap: 500% markup- **Buy separately:** Home Depot, U-Haul, Amazon

**Insurance upgrades:**

- Full value protection- **Cost:** 1-2% of declared value- **Reality:** High deductibles, claim difficulties- **Alternative:** Homeowners policy review

**Storage coordination:**

- Mover arranges storage- **Cost:** Markup on storage rates- **Better:** Direct storage rental

## Geographic Specific Services

**LA-specific valuable services:**

**Parking permits:**

- City permit applications- **Cost:** $40-100- **Value:** Prevents tickets/delays

**Building coordination:**

- Elevator reservations- COI processing- **Cost:** $50-100- **Value:** Smooth building access

**Traffic routing:**

- Avoiding congestion- **Cost:** Included with good companies- **Value:** Prevents delays/overtime

## Timing-Based Services

**Express/guaranteed delivery:**

- Specific delivery dates- **Cost:** 25-50% premium- **Value:** Schedule certainty- **Consider:** Business/school deadlines

**Weekend moving:**

- Saturday/Sunday service- **Cost:** 20-30% premium- **Value:** Convenience- **Alternative:** Friday/weekday savings

**After-hours moving:**

- Evening/night moves- **Cost:** 25-40% premium- **Necessity:** Building restrictions sometimes

## Specialty Packing Services

**Custom crating:**

- Artwork/antiques- **Cost:** $200-500 per item- **When needed:** Irreplaceable items- **Skip for:** Replaceable furniture

**Electronics packing:**

- Professional techniques- **Cost:** $25-50 per item- **DIY option:** Original boxes + care

**Document handling:**

- File organization- **Cost:** $35-45/hour- **Better:** Handle personally (confidentiality)

## Assembly Services Breakdown

**Worth professional assembly:**

- Complex furniture (IKEA wardrobes)- Exercise equipment- Play sets- **Cost:** $25-40/hour- **Value:** Expertise prevents errors

**DIY assembly:**

- Simple furniture- Bookshelves- Basic tables- **Savings:** $100-300

## Moving Insurance Reality

**Basic coverage (included):**

- $0.60 per pound- Essentially worthless- Legal minimum only

**Full value protection:**

- Replacement cost coverage- **Cost:** 1-2% of declared value- **Deductibles:** $250-500 typical- **Claims:** Often disputed

**Homeowner's policy:**

- May cover moving damage- Review before purchasing extra

## Service Bundling Strategies

**Smart bundles:**

- Basic move + disassembly + materials- Packing (fragile items only) + wardrobe boxes

**Avoid bundles:**

- Full service everything- Cleaning + storage + supplies- "White glove" packages

**Negotiate:** Individual service pricing

## DIY vs Professional Decision Tree

**Pack yourself:**

- Clothes, books, non-fragiles- **Savings:** $1,000+- **Time required:** 20-40 hours

**Professional packing for:**

- Kitchen dishes- Artwork/antiques- Complex electronics- **Cost:** $300-800- **Value:** Damage prevention

## Questions to Ask

**Service evaluation questions:**

- "What's included in base rate?"- "What are individual service costs?"- "Can I skip services I don't need?"- "What's the minimum service level?"- "Are there package discounts?"

**Avoid companies that:**

- Force unnecessary services- Won't itemize costs- Use pressure tactics

## Budget Optimization

**Maximum value approach:**

- Essential services only- DIY what's reasonable- Professional for fragile/complex- **Total savings:** 30-50%

**Time-saving approach:**

- More professional services- Less DIY involvement- **Premium:** 25-40%- **Value:** Convenience

## Service Quality Indicators

**Professional service includes:**

- Clear explanations- Itemized pricing- No pressure tactics- Flexible options- Transparent policies

**Red flags:**

- Mandatory expensive services- Package-only pricing- High-pressure sales- Vague descriptions

## Make Smart Service Choices

Moving services range from essential to exploitative. Smart selection focuses on services preventing damage, saving significant time, or providing expertise you lack. Skip services that cost more than doing yourself or hiring specialists directly.
