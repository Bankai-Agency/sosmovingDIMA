Usually, when you’re moving, you need to start packing as early as possible. How much time you will need depends on the size of your household, the distance between your two houses, your packing speed, whether you’re hiring [SOS Moving LA](https://sosmovingla.net/) or not, and so on. Generally speaking, the usual time frame would be two months in advance. This gives you enough time to pack everything.** If you are in a hurry, you will have a higher possibility of misplacing, damaging, or losing something.** However, sometimes, you do not have enough time for packing. You might even have to organize your reallocation in less than a week. For this reason, here are all the best last-minute move packing tips.

## Make a plan for last-minute move packing 

Even though you do not have enough time to prepare everything, you still need to have a decent plan. If you start packing your items without any agenda, you might find yourself in a huge problem after a couple of days. For this reason, take a half-hour and work out all the details of your packing plan. See if you want to hire [packing services Los Angeles](https://sosmovingla.net/services/packing-services/) so movers can pack everything instead of you or you want to deal with everything by yourself. After this, based on your decision, *you should organize your packing schedule*. The first thing you should do is to look for a moving company. Usually, you should also hire A moving company a couple of weeks in advance. However, if you do not have enough time, there are many moving companies that do accept the last-minute booking.

## Declutter your items 

You might think you do not have enough time to declutter your items. However, you should declutter your items and throw away all the things that you do not need. First of all, **you will save time packing if you have a smaller number of items to pack.** Then, you will also save money because [long distance movers Los Angeles](https://sosmovingla.net/services/long-distance-move/) will have a small number of boxes to transport. Because of this, take one day off and declutter everything. You have several choices for items that you no longer need. Here are all of them.

- **Sell** – if you have enough time, you can try selling your items. - **Donate** – you can apply for tax reduction afterward. - **Give to someone** – give your items for free to your friends and family members. - **Throw away** – all the items in bad condition should be thrown away. 

## Put together an essential box for last-minute move packing

Since you do not have much time, you cannot exactly pack everything the way you want. For this reason, your boxes might be filled with random items that you wouldn’t usually place together. In order to avoid confusion and being without your essential items for the first couple of days after you move in, you should *pack one or maybe more essential boxes/bags*. If you are going to hire [movers Palmdale CA](https://sosmovingla.net/palmdale-movers/) to transport your items, you won’t be able to use them until they are delivered. For this reason, you need to have all of your essentials next to you. Usually, this is what you need to put in your essential box.

- A change of clothes- Medications- Toiletries- Documents- Your laptop and charges- Anything else you think is necessary

## Be efficient when packing 

As mentioned before, you do not have time to pack every room separately. You also do not have time to group similar items together. For this reason, you only need to pack as quickly as possible. Do not pay attention to organizing things or grouping them together. **You need to pay attention to make your items safe and secure during transport.** This is important because you do not want to cause any damage to your items during your relocation. You can organize everything and sort everything properly later once you move into your new house. In addition to this, you can use bubble wrapping to secure your items. However, if you do not have enough, you can always use your socks, towels, bed sheet to wrap your breakables. This way, you will pack both your breakables with your other items.
