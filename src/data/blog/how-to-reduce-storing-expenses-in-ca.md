### Share the storage space

Try to find someone to share storage space with. Depending on their storage needs, you may end up paying less for the same amount of storage, because you’re sharing the expenses. It’s important to remember that only one of your names will be going up on the lease, so choose carefully who you share the storage space with to prevent problems down the road. This option has as many pros as it has cons. You will also have to [keep your storage unit organized](https://www.moving.com/tips/how-to-organize-a-storage-unit-like-a-pro/) and ask the person you’re sharing the storage with to do the same.
