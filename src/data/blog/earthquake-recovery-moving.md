## LA Seismic Reality

**Major fault lines:**

- San Andreas (most dangerous)- Hollywood fault- Santa Monica fault- Newport-Inglewood fault- Puente Hills thrust

- 6.7+ magnitude: 60% within 30 years- 7.0+ magnitude: 46% chance- Major disaster: When not if

## Immediate Post-Earthquake Protocol

**First 24 hours priorities:**

- Personal safety assessment- Structural damage evaluation- Utility shutoffs if needed- Emergency services contact- Insurance notification- Documentation begins

**Do not enter severely damaged buildings**

**Professional assessment required before re-entry**

**Critical timeline:**

- Hours 0-24: Safety and triage- Hours 24-48: Assessment and planning- Hours 48-72: Recovery begins

**After 72 hours:**

- Secondary damage possible- Mold concerns begin- Security issues increase- Trauma deepens

**Professional intervention within 72 hours optimal**
