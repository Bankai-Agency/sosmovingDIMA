## Types of Eviction Situations

**Court-ordered evictions:**Legal process completed. Sheriff notice posted. Lock-out date scheduled. No negotiation possible. Must vacate completely. Time running out.

**Cash-for-keys agreements:**Landlord pays to leave. Negotiated departure date. Avoid eviction record. Moving money provided. Faster for everyone. Dignity preserved somewhat.

**Unofficial pressure:**Harassment tactics used. Utilities shut off. Locks changed illegally. Intimidation constant. Rights violated. Help needed urgently.

**Foreclosure evictions:**Property sold/foreclosed. New owner evicting. Tenant rights limited. Short notice given. Previous agreements void. Displacement sudden.
