## New or used moving boxes?

Deciding if you want to get used or new moving boxes is important since it will help you decide on the moving budget. Old moving boxes might be a great solution if you get them for one portion of your belongings. The things that are not fragile are a **great example of when you can use used moving boxes**.* Your clothes, stuffed animals, and similar items can be easily moved in old moving boxes since they are not heavy, or fragile.* But, for your fragile, breakable, and important belongings, new moving boxes are the right solution. If you get a [packing service](https://sosmovingla.net/services/packing-services/), they will find it much easier to pack everything in new moving boxes. So, this decision is up to you.
