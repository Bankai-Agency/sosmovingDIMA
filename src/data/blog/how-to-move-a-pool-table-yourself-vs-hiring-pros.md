That 8-foot pool table looks manageable until you learn the slate alone weighs 800 pounds and costs $3,000 to replace when it cracks. The DIY vs professional debate ends quickly when you understand physics, liability, and the very real possibility of destroying your $5,000 table. Here's why moving a pool table yourself ranks among the worst DIY decisions possible.

## Pool Table Anatomy Lesson

**What you're actually moving:**

- Slate bed: 700-1,000 pounds- Frame: 200-300 pounds- Rails and pockets: 50 pounds- Felt: Delicate, tears easily- Total weight: 1,000-1,300 pounds

**Slate specifications:**

- 3/4" to 1" thick- Usually 3 pieces- Precisely leveled- Cracks if flexed- Cannot be repaired

**One crack = table destroyed**
