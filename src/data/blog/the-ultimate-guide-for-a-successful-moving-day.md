You have probably heard that relocation is a stressful process and that you should prepare yourself for that. However, this does not have to be the case with your move. You just need to** plan steps in advance and everything will run smoothly**. As a matter of fact, we are going to provide you with a helpful guide and you will know what to do and how to do it. Even though there are many things to do, do not worry – you will be ready to handle everything and it will be a successful moving day.

## Once your movers arrive, check the important parts with them

When your movers come to your home, it is important that you go through the whole moving plan. Just to be on the safe side, ask them how your moving day is going to look like. If it happens that you would like for some minorities to be done in a different way,* feel free to ask them.* If it happens that [SOS Moving and Storage Los Angeles](https://sosmovingla.net/) is your moving partner, you can be sure that they are going to do their best to meet all of your moving needs.
![A woman labeling a box](../../assets/cdn/645ab1d97922878b6f5bef7f/645ab1d9792287c2395bf47f_pexels-ketut-subiyanto-4246196-e1600103285580.jpeg)*Check with your movers when you can expect your belongings to arrive at your new home*
In addition to this, you should also ask about how many days they will need to deliver your belongings. If it happens that you are moving long-distance, try to be flexible. They will probably need two or three days,  but you should also be aware of the fact that there are factors that can change the schedule for the driver.

## An essentials bag should be near you

You have probably added [packing an essentials bag](https://www.moving.com/tips/moving-essentials-bag-what-you-need-to-pack/) to your moving checklist. It is important to pack it because you can never be sure whether you will unpack everything you need on the first day or not. For this reason,** it is always preferable to have all the necessary items at hand**. Once you pack this bag, put it somewhere where you can reach it easily but it should also not stand in the way. In addition to this, you should carry an essentials bag with you. Do not put it in a moving truck unless you are moving locally and you know that your movers will bring your belongings the same day. Also, do not forget to pack linen, toiletries, charger, plastic cups, and some clothes in this bag.

## For a successful moving day, you should have a plan for your children

In case your children are still little, you should think about who will be around them. If you are [relocating locally](https://sosmovingla.net/services/local-move/), leave them at your parent’s or your friend’s house. If it happens that they have the time to take care of them, it will be a perfect solution. However, if this is not an option, you can always find a babysitter. You should **check this on time and organize everything beforehand**.
![Family](../../assets/cdn/645ab1d97922878b6f5bef7f/645ab1d97922877bf35bf483_pexels-emma-bauso-2253879-e1600103454536.jpeg)*In order to have a successful moving day, you should think about how to keep your children occupied*
What is also important is to make sure their favorite items are with them. This will minimize the stress and they will feel safer. On the other hand, if your children are older, you can feel free to involve them in the moving process. For example, they can take care of their own room, that is, they can unpack some items of their own.

## Get rid of everything that you are not allowed to put in a moving truck

You probably already know what items you cannot put in a moving truck. In case you do not, feel free to get in touch with your mover and ask for a complete list. After that, what you should do in order to have a successful moving day is to **get rid of them before the moving day comes**, especially if you are [moving long-distance](https://sosmovingla.net/services/long-distance-movers/). If these as well as other items for garbage pile up, it will only be an additional task to complete. Avoid this from happening since you will already have a lot of things to do. Of course, it is possible that there will still be some trash, but one bag is not the same as five.

## Prepare everything for your movers

One more thing that is important to do in order to have a successful moving day is preparing for the movers. This means that you should clear the way throughout your home. Basically,** you should remove all the potted plants that are near doors**. In addition to this, it is also preferable to remove all doors and doormats. If the weather is not pleasant, feel free to put some cardboard or old blankets on the floor.
![Beverages](../../assets/cdn/645ab1d97922878b6f5bef7f/645ab1d97922870a095bf48a_pexels-elevate-3009754-e1600103750817.jpeg)*Offer beverages and snacks to your movers from time to time, especially if you are moving during the summer*
Another thing that you can do is take care of your crew members. Either if you are relocating your home or you are [moving your office](https://sosmovingla.net/services/commercial-move/), your movers should **always have enough water and some snacks**. If you have the time, feel free even to buy them some lunch. Having a break is important and when you treat your movers well, they know how to pay it back.

It is true that relocation is an overwhelming process. However,* this does not mean that it is a stressful one as well*. This part depends largely on you- if you prepare everything on time, you can be sure that you will have a successful moving day. Put these pieces of advice into practice and you will have nothing to worry about. Everything will run smoothly and you will get to remember your moving day with a smile on your face.
