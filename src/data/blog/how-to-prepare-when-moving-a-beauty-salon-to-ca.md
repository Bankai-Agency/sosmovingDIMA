## Prepare when moving a beauty salon to CA: Planning your move

A good moving plan comes in two phases. Phase one is to create a [moving budget](https://www.wikihow.com/Create-a-Budget). This will help you organize *expenses, manage your income, and keep your finances in check*. More importantly, it will allow you to know **exactly how much you can spend on your move**, and how much on your beauty salon. This is the single most important thing you can do to prepare yourself for the next relocation. Phase two is to find a good place where you are going to relocate your beauty salon. Now, since you are reading this article that must mean you are searching for real estate in the Cali area. First, check your moving budget – are you going to rent or buy? Second, check the internet for real estate in the area you are looking to relocate to. Third, contact potential sellers and schedule a meet-up.
