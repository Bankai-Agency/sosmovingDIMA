## Seasonal Price Variations

**Peak season (May-September):**

- Rates increase 20-30%- Minimum hours increase- Availability decreases- Book 3-4 weeks ahead

**Off-season (November-February):**

- Better rates available- More flexibility- Same-week booking possible- Negotiate better deals

**Month-end premium:**Everyone moves on the 31st/1st. Rates jump 25-40%. Move mid-month = save money.
