### Take pictures of cables and cords setups when packing your game room for relocation

However, before you can safely pack everything, you might want to **double-check where all the cables go**. You will want to **take pictures** of every device that is plugged in, noting the cable and cord setups. You may wish to recreate them in your new home, after all.
![plugged in cables](../../assets/cdn/645ab1d97922878b6f5bef7f/645ab1d979228749995bf3c7_pexels-brett-sayles-1624895-scaled-e1613511393204.jpeg)*Take a picture of your cable setup before you disconnect.*
Another way of doing this is by *labeling the cables themselves*. Simply strap a piece of paper on each cable, noting where it goes. This will allow you to easily reconnect everything after you arrive at your new place. Otherwise, *you might be looking at several hours of trying to figure out what goes where*.
