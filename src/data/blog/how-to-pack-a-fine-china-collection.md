## Wash and clean your collection properly 

If your fine China collection is pretty big, it means that you are not probably using every single piece daily. For this reason, you need to properly clean everything before you decide to pack them. You should never pack items that are dusty, or dirty and this also includes your China. By now, you should already know how to properly [clean your fine China collection](https://www.diynetwork.com/how-to/maintenance-and-repair/cleaning/how-to-clean-china-and-crystal). If you decide just to wipe the dust, make sure to do it early. On the other hand, if you decide to wash it with water and soap, make sure they are properly dry before putting them inside the box. You should never **pack items that are slightly wet**. Everything should be completely dry.

If not, then mold can develop on your items and cause huge damage not only to the item on it but also to everything else.   

## Steps to take when you need to pack a fine China collection 

Now that you have everything ready and prepared, it is time to actually pack your China pieces. Start by using bubble wrapping. Make sure you have enough because you need to wrap every single item that you own. You should also do it separately. This way, you will ensure that all the pieces will be well protected and safe during transport. Once you use bubble wrapping, you need to safely secure it with the tape. Then, put all the wrapped pieces inside the box is. Make sure not to overstuff the box so it will not break during transport. In addition to this, you need to fill the empty space inside the box so your wrapped pieces will not be able to move. You can use *packing peanuts for this purpose*. After everything, make sure to label the box so everyone will know that fine China is inside.
