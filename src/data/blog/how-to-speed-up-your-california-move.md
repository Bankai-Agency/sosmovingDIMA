## Planning ahead will get you a long way

One of the best ways to reach your goal is to make a plan in advance. This means that you should [make a moving checklist](https://www.moving.com/move-planner/) before you actually start completing the tasks. **Feel free to be as detailed as possible** because this can mean a lot during the moving process.
![Pen on to-do list](../../assets/cdn/645ab1d97922878b6f5bef7f/645ab1d97922874e815bf351_pexels-breakingpic-3243-e1619512294287.jpeg)*Make a moving checklist and you will speed up your California move*
For example, if you know that alongside one major task you should complete several minor tasks, do not rely on your memory. You will have a lot of things on your mind and it can easily happen that you forget about those minor tasks. So, what you should do is make them part of your moving checklist and you will be completely covered.

## You will speed up your California move if you get rid of some of your items

As we have already stated, the packing part can last for a long span of time. In order to make it shorter, **feel free to give or throw away some of your items**.
