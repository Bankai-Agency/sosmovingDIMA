## How do you pack fragile items for relocation?

Once you got everything you need to pack all your belongings, you can just relax and start packing one item at a time. *The first thing you have to do is choose the right box for each of the items you will be packing.* Start with the ones that are larger since your focus is the best at the beginning of the job. **Here is how you pack fragile items:**

- Pick an item you want to pack- Get the packing peanuts and put them at the bottom of the box you have chosen. It needs to be just a bit larger than the item you are about to pack- Once you choose it, get the packing peanuts and place them at the bottom of your box- Place the item on top of the packing peanuts- Use the packing peanuts and packing paper to protect the item from all the sides. If you feel like there is a need for it, get clothes and blankets and additionally protect the item inside the box- Once you are sure that the item can’t move in any direction, place the packing peanuts on top of the item until the box is filled- Shut the box and tape it with the duct tape so it can’t get opened- Label it with the marker, and make sure you write that there is something fragile inside

You can be sure that this way, your belongings will be more than safe for this relocation. You just have to be careful **not to break the items while you are packing them.*** Plates can be packed more than one peace at a time, just like glasses and mugs, but you have to wrap each one and make a barrier in between.*
