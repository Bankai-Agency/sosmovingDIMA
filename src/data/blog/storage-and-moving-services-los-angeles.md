## Climate-Controlled vs Standard Storage

**Climate control necessary for:**Wood furniture preventing warping. Electronics avoiding moisture damage. Artwork maintaining integrity. Leather goods preventing cracking. Documents avoiding mildew. Musical instruments preserving tune. Photographs preventing sticking. Wine collections maintaining quality.

**Standard storage sufficient for:**Outdoor furniture designed for weather. Plastic and metal items. Non-sensitive equipment. Seasonal decorations. Tools and hardware. Most kitchenware. Exercise equipment. Garden supplies.

LA's moderate climate makes standard storage viable for many items, unlike humid regions requiring universal climate control.
