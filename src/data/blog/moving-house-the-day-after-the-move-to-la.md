## Unpack large pieces of furniture

This is precisely where you should start. You should know precisely where you would like to put what item. First of all, these pieces will no longer be in your way and you will also make room for other items. In case you are reassembling your items on your own, **get a screwdriver and start completing the task**.

## Large appliances should be installed the day after the move to LA

In case you have brought with you the appliances from your old home, *you should install them*. Naturally, you can count on the most professional [appliance movers Los Angeles](https://sosmovingla.net/services/appliance-move/) can offer. Check the manuals if you need some help with installation. Feel free to start from your kitchen. **Install your fridge, stove, dishwasher, and washer and dryer**. Once you do this, you will make life in your new home much easier. It will be possible for you to start using your kitchen and everything will be functional.

## Unpack your bathroom and bedrooms

The next parts of your home you should unpack are your bathroom and bedrooms. You have probably unpacked the essential items but the time has come to unpack the rest. So, get the boxes with your cleaning supplies, toiletries, etc., and think about where to put them in your cabinets.
