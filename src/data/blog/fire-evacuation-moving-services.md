## Fire Evacuation Zones Explained

**Mandatory evacuation (RED):**

- Leave NOW- No time for movers- Life over belongings- Roads closing

**Evacuation warning (ORANGE):**

- Prepare to leave- 6-48 hours typically- Movers possible- Priority evacuation

**Evacuation watch (YELLOW):**

- Monitor closely- Start preparations- Schedule movers- High alert

**Professional movers work in ORANGE zones only.**
