Mover’s license is proof that specific requirements are met. Primarily, those are [moving insurance](https://www.forbes.com/advisor/homeowners-insurance/moving-insurance/), financial responsibility, and safety measures. A reliable moving company will have *no problem providing its license and certifications*.
