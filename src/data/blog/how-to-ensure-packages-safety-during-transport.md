## How to prepare your items for packing?

Packing your items doesn’t only mean putting them in boxes or wrapping them with packing paper. Yes, these are all important steps, but there is something you should do before that. **Preparation is the key to any success**, and it’s also a key move in packing. Some items require more attention than others, and usually, these items are furniture and other larger belongings. For this reason, you should [measure your furniture](https://www.weekendsonly.com/furniture-measuring-guide), disassemble, and pack every piece separately.
