## Los Angeles-Specific Reminders

**Parking and permits:**LA parking enforcement is aggressive. Permits required for moving trucks. Street cleaning tickets are $73. Beach communities have special restrictions. Downtown requires building coordination.

**Utility considerations:**LADWP requires deposits. SoCal Gas separate service. Spectrum vs Frontier availability varies. Solar panels need transfer coordination. Pool service arrangements.

**Emergency preparedness:**Earthquake kit essential. Wildfire evacuation plans. Tsunami zones (beach areas). Power outage supplies. Water storage recommended.
