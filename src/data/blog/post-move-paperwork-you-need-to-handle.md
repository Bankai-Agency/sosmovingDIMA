The relocation process is finally over. You have completed all of the before-the-move tasks but it is still not the time to relax completely. There are just a few more tasks left to complete. Namely, you need to handle some post-move paperwork. You have nothing to worry about since **we are here to remind you of all the important things**. There is no reason to stress yourself since we are going to point out what are the most important steps you should take.

## Making a list is a good way to go

Since you have probably made a moving checklist before your relocation, you know how helpful this is. When post-move paperwork is in question, the list will be different, of course. Namely, the paperwork that should be updated and handled is the following:

- medical records,- school records,- your address and phone number,- home services and utilities.
![A woman making a list](../../assets/cdn/645ab1d97922878b6f5bef7f/645ab1d979228703535bf478_pexels-kaboompics-com-6360-e1602343569655.jpeg)*Make a list of all of the paperwork you should handle and you will complete this task successfully and on time*
Just bear in mind that when medical records are in question, you should remind your whole family to transfer them. If it happens that you are [relocating long-distance](https://sosmovingla.net/services/long-distance-move/), it is preferable to complete this task before the move. Just go to the doctor, inform them about your relocation and they will give you all the paperwork. In fact, you can even ask them if they can recommend a certain doctor to you.

## What are the ways in which you can get things done?

Considering the fact that almost everything is digitalized, it is possible that you can simplify the process of transferring your documents. This means that, first of all, **you should give your bank a call**. Inform them about changing your place of residence and ask whether you can change your address by phone. If you can, you will complete this task in a short span of time. The same goes when your home services are in question if you have [relocated locally](https://sosmovingla.net/services/local-move/). When your children’s school records are in question, check if you can transfer them online. Go to their website and search for this option. In case you cannot find it, contact their school and they will give you the instructions.

## Changing your address is something you should not delay

Even though nowadays a great majority of things are digitalized, there are some matters that can be handled only in person. For example, changing your address in one of them. This means that you should not wait for your relocation and then change it. Instead, **complete this task even before your moving day comes.** Remember to put it on your moving checklist and you will have nothing to worry about.
![A mailbox](../../assets/cdn/645ab1d97922878b6f5bef7f/645ab1d97922876fd15bf476_pexels-sean-patrick-1046403-e1602343705986.jpeg)*Another one of the post-move paperwork you need to handle is changing your address*
Your [moving day will be successful](https://sosmovingla.net/the-ultimate-guide-for-a-successful-moving-day/) and the whole process will run smoothly. In case you do not do this on time, you will be in a very inconvenient situation. It is even possible that you will have to come back which will be only an additional waste of your time and money. So, do everything by the book and you will avoid this scenario from happening.

## If you are moving to a different state, handle post-move paperwork on time

You already know that this kind of relocation is quite a complex one. All of the relocation-related tasks should be completed on time and with success. The same goes for your personal documents. This means that you should not wait for your relocation to take place and then handle the paperwork. Instead, [let your movers pack your belongings](https://sosmovingla.net/services/packing-services/) while you are completing this task. This is a good thing for two reasons. First of all, professionals will deal with all of your items and they will pay special attention to the fragile ones. Second,** you will not be pressed for time and everything will be prepared without delay**. However, when your driver’s license is in question, this is something you cannot change before the move. You will have to wait until you relocate and then get a new one.

## In case you are moving to a different country, there are other documents you should bear in mind

Making a plan in advance is the key to success. The situation is the same when you are preparing your documents when relocating to a different country. When going through this process, **you should check whether your passport and visas are valid**. In addition to this, if you are [relocating with a pet](https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/international-travel/before-you-go/travelers-with-special-considerations/taking-a-pet-overseas.html), you should get a passport for it as well. Also, you should also get a veterinarian’s certificate that they are healthy.
![A dog and a cat](../../assets/cdn/645ab1d97922878b6f5bef7f/645ab1d979228786af5bf484_pexels-snapwire-46024-e1602343787807.jpeg)*If it happens that you are moving with a pet to a different country, you should get them a passport and a certificate from the vet*
What you should also pay attention to is your driver’s license. Even if it is valid, it is possible that in some countries it is not valid. Check this on time and you will know what to do. In case you have a car and you would like to have it transported, you will have to authorize a car carrier to do this on your behalf.
