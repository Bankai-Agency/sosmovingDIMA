## Send each other presents or care packages

The other great way to keep in touch would be to send each other presents or care packages. When you are living so far away from each other, the best way to show that you are still thinking about your friends and family would be to *send them a small present*. This does not have to be anything big or flashy. Just think about something that your friends and family would like to have. The best present would be something that would remind your friends and family of you. In addition to this, it would be better if you do not tell your friends or family that you are sending them a present so it would be a surprise for them when they receive it. If you do not have any ideas, you can always look up the [best present ideas](https://www.tellinga.com/post/19-best-long-distance-relationship-gifts-for-your-bae) on the Internet.

There are plenty of beautiful cities between New York and California. You can choose a city where you have never been before. So, you can explore a new city,** meet with your friends and family**, and have a good time. As you can see, this would be a perfect opportunity to explore other cities in the area. You can also welcome your friends and family to your new house and show them around your new city.
