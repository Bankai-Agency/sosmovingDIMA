## What can be an essential bag?

It is your wish that you choose to be the place where you will **keep your key things when moving interstate.** It can usually be a box like any other where you will put all your essentials next to each other. However, as water and other liquids can often be here, a plastic box with partitions is also recommended, where you will separate everything nicely. Be sure to **mark the box where you keep your essentials** for the moving day so that your people from [packing services Los Angeles](https://sosmovingla.net/services/packing-services/) would not be confused with the rest of your belongings.

You can also use a smaller or larger bag, which is easier to carry on the go. The most important thing is to be careful where your essential bag is all the time, and not to lose sight of it, because that is exactly the point of packing a box or bag like this. *Packing the bag of essentials for your interstate relocation is an important thing and will help you a lot on the day of moving and after.*

**Keeping sensitive and important documents safe is important while you move interstate.** Everything that is of great importance from your documents should be with you when you move interstate. There’s nothing worse than the feeling of your phone running out of battery and not being able to find your charger, especially during a big move when you’re likely coordinating with your new landlord, family members, moving company, and any others involved in the process.  **Make sure to pack your phone charger, along with any other important electronics**, so they don’t get lost or stolen during the move. If you are moving your business, your [office movers Los Angeles](https://sosmovingla.net/services/commercial-move/) will pack all your expensive equipment, so it is important that you keep something important to you.

## Medications – first thing when packing the bag of essentials for your interstate move

**Medication is very important to be a part of packing essential bag when relocating interstate**. Moving can be difficult for the body and head, so it is advisable to bring some painkillers if this happens. For example, take sleeping pills with you to rest before unpacking. Also, *if you already have a prescribed therapy that you take regularly, make sure it is in your bag *so that you can drink it regardless of the duration of the move. Also, this is a good option. ## Pet items

If you’re moving interstate with pets, it’s a good idea to **pack them their own essentials bag. **Make sure to include a portable water dish, a couple of days of food, some toys as needed, and any medications. You might also want to check in with your vet before you move to see how you can ease the transition for your furry friend. As they could have some good recommendations for your particular pet.

When moving into a new space, it’s best not to assume that there will be toilet paper waiting for you when you get there. Packing that extra roll provides some peace of mind. When you are *packing the bag of essentials things for your interstate move, this must be one of the more significant things.*

In case you don’t want to rummage through your boxes when you first arrive in your new place, we recommend also packing a change of clothes. That includes a full outfit, socks, and underwear!

**Moving is tiring work, both physically and emotionally**.
