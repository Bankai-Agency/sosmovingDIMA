It is always extremely exciting to move into your new home. This is especially true if you have finally found a house that **meets all of your requirements**. However, since you are used to living in your old house, you might find a couple of things in your new house that you don’t like. Once the [best moving companies Los Angeles](https://sosmovingla.net/) deliver your items, you should do something about this. Usually, people tend to redecorate and even renovate their new home. If you don’t have enough time or you simply do not want to deal with all the renovation, here are simple decorating tips that you can try once you relocate to Los Angeles.

## Simple decorating tips – enhance the exterior of the house

Well, they say that you should not judge a book by its cover. This is certainly true. However, when you see a beautiful house from the outside, you probably think that the interior must be breathtaking as well. For this reason, you should **enhance the exterior of your house**. Start with your front door. If you feel a little bit adventurous, you can paint the front door in a fun color. [Moving companies in Westwood](https://sosmovingla.net/westwood-movers/) will definitely recognize your house due to blue or yellow doors. Mind you, this does not have to be an extremely bright color,* but rather soft yellow or blue*. In addition to this, if you have a small front garden, you can **plant a couple of pretty flowers**. You can even match the color of flowers with your front door or make a striking contrast.

## Repaint your interior as well

Since you are already repainting parts of your house, you should also do the interior walls as well. This is especially important if the previous owners of the house had a peculiar taste in colors. Living in a bright red room will certainly cause a headache. For this reason, you should **repaint your walls in more suitable colors**. Most people tend to gravitate toward natural colors as it is easier to decorate your rooms with these colors. In addition to this, if you are moving to a smaller apartment with [Los Angeles apartment movers](https://sosmovingla.net/services/apartment-move/), then the lack of space will definitely be a problem. Having neutral colors will make your space look much bigger than it really is. You can add splashes of colors with **different types of decorations**. This can include pillows, carpet, sofa, curtains, etc.

## Simple decorating tips – arrange your living room

Usually, people spend most of their time in the living room. For this reason, you should make this room both comfortable and pretty to look at. First of all, you can buy a sofa that is in** a different color than the rest of your furniture**. This will add color to your neutrally painted room. Then, you can also buy an L-shape sofa if you often entertain guests in your house. This way, you will have plenty of sitting areas. In addition to this, do not forget to add *the coffee table and also two chairs*. The coffee table should be both functional and aesthetically pleasing. As for the chairs, you can play with colors here as well and **buy chairs in some bright colors**. You do not have to worry about bringing these pieces of furniture into your house if you hire [moving labor Los Angeles](https://sosmovingla.net/services/labor-only-services/).

## Renovate your kitchen

In addition to the living room, your kitchen is probably one of the most important rooms in your house. Here, you will spend most of your time cooking for your family or for your guests. There is nothing more relaxing than cooking your favorite meals in **a well-arranged kitchen**. However, at the same time, if your kitchen is not suitable for your taste, it can be quite depressing to make meals. For this reason, you should let the sunshine in your kitchen by *upgrading or even creating new windows*. You should do this before [appliance movers Los Angeles](https://sosmovingla.net/services/appliance-move/) deliver your kitchen appliances. You would not want to damage your expensive kitchen appliances due to all the renovation happening in your kitchen. Lastly, if you keep your kitchen color-neutral while adding a couple of colorful points,** your kitchen will look spectacular**.

## Simple decorating tips – mirrors

Mirror, mirror on the wall, who is the precious of them all? That would be your house. Your house is not complete without having at **least one mirror in each room**. This might be one of the simplest decorating suggestions. The mirrors in your house are not only for vanity purposes but for [decorating purposes](https://decoholic.org/professional-tips-for-decorating-walls-with-mirrors/). First of all, if your rooms are quite small, placing a mirror inside, it would make your room appear much larger than it is. It is important to make your house *feel spacious and comfortable*. In addition to this, mirrors also help to light up your room. If you are struggling with natural light, **place mirrors perpendicular to the window** to achieve this effect. Do not be afraid to play with shapes and the size of the mirrors. You can find cheap yet effective mirrors at your local stores.

## The last set of suggestions

Lastly, here are more decorating tips for your new house that you should try after your relocation.

- **Place artwork on your wall** – this will definitely add a personal touch and create a comfortable atmosphere in your home.- **Layer your lighting** – use all the available sources of lighting in your home, from a small desk lamp to the torchiere.- **Place carpets under furniture** – the carpet should define the sitting area. For this reason, place all the furniture partially or completely on the carpet.- **Declutter your house** – pretty simple yet extremely effective. Get rid of all the clutter and unnecessary items.- **Replace old fixtures** – a true Cinderella treatment. Replace all the old fixtures around your house. In addition to this, replace anything old or that you do not like in your new house.
