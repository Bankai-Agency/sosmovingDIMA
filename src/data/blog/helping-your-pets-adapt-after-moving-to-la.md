When you’re moving, be it a local or long-distance move, your pets will surely make the way to your new home. However,** not every pet will react the same to a move.** Some will remain playful and curious even after coming to LA, but some will turn shy and hide. In order to help our [local movers Los Angeles](https://sosmovingla.net/) have some tips to make your pets adapt after moving to LA in no time. *Here are just some of the things you can do to make your pet feel more at home.*

## Give your pets time to adapt after moving to LA

When you finally unpack everything it’s important to give your pets time. Your new home will be a big unknown for your pets. For that reason, **it’s very important that you leave them time and space to get to know their new environment.** For example, our [movers California to Colorado](https://sosmovingla.net/services/interstate-move/california-to-colorado/) will know best how hard it is for animals to make this transition. Especially one where even the climate changes. Keep an eye on them and see where they feel comfortable. That’s where you should make space dedicated to them. *Most pets will have a negative reaction to relocating, so having patience and giving them time to adapt is very important.*

## Pamper pets with their favorite treats and toys

**The best way to help pets adapt after relocating to LA is to keep their feeding and playing schedule the same as before.** However, that shouldn’t stop you from showing some extra love to them. *Keeping their old toys and bed will speed up the process of them feeling better in the new home.* Even our [residential movers LA](https://sosmovingla.net/services/residential-move/) know that a good treat will make any pet happy. This is a bad time to experiment with new stuff, so keep it simple and familiar. Especially if your pet is agitated or feeling down, a sweet will cheer it up.

## Keep your pets safe in your new LA home

It’s not a rare occurrence that right after the move pets get scared and change their behavior. *Try and create a safe space for your pet as soon as possible.* If it helps your pets adapt after moving to LA even call [appliance movers Los Angeles](https://sosmovingla.net/services/appliance-move/) to move things around your house. Make sure your pet doesn’t run away, because it can happen when they feel afraid, and the best way to do so is by making them feel good in the new home. If you want to make sure your pet is safe even outside, ask about potential aggressive animals around the neighborhood.

## Love and attention will help pets adapt after relocating to Los Angeles

Use every moment of free time to stay at home with your pets. Showing them that they got your full attention and care will make them feel safer in unfamiliar conditions. If your pet becomes aggressive or shy that doesn’t mean it will stay like that forever. When you have in mind all the [positive effects of pets on their owners](https://www.cdc.gov/healthypets/health-benefits/index.html) it shouldn’t be a big deal for you to **show them more affection than usual** for a couple of days or maybe weeks.

In the end, *every animal will behave differently when relocating.* Even if you as the owner can feel stressed when relocating, you can only imagine how hard it can be for an animal. They didn’t plan weeks or months in advance like you, they were just in a new home all of a sudden. However, **we are sure that your pets will adapt quickly after moving to LA if you follow our advice.** We hope that both you and your pets will enjoy your new place in LA!
