## What to include in the pre-move measuring checklist

It doesn’t matter what kind of relocation you are planning, like small, big, international, local, etc. You will always have to do this before you move out. So, **here are the items and stuff you have to measure when moving:**

- How far and wide are the doorsteps- Pay close attention to staircases- Distances between the doors and walls- **Measure all the big and bulky belongings you have like furniture and other items**- If you can, disassemble and then measure some furniture pieces- Take measures of the vehicles you are using if you are transporting the furniture by yourself.

## Why is measuring important

As you already know, not only will you be able to gather all the packing supplies you will need for your move, but you will have an opportunity to **plan your interior design** in your new home. And all that before you move in. This step is really important because it will [make trailering easier](https://www.gmc.com/trailering-towing). Trailering is a process where you will distribute your heavy items properly in the moving truck. It is something you need to do to avoid any kind of problems and issues when driving. It is a safety precaution advised by many skilled transporters.
![a measuring tape](../../assets/cdn/645ab1d97922878b6f5bef7f/645ab1d979228762615bf3a8_measurement-1476919_1280.jpeg)*With measuring, we can plan the interior of our new home*
When you have proper measurements of all the items you want to move, you will know **how big of a moving truck to ask for. **And by that, we mean the trailer. If you take the much bigger trailer, then you will have to pay a lot more for your move. Too small, and you **won’t move your items**. Knowing the right measures will allow you to get just the right type and size of the moving truck trailer. Make sure to do this before you move out if you wish to have a nice and secure move.
