Frequently Asked Questions

What is a white-glove moving service? What services do white glove movers offer?

White glove movers at SOS Moving & Storage provide a full range of elite moving services to help you move securely and carefully. The services include custom packing, custom crating, furniture assembly and disassembly, transportation, installation, and storage. Our experts also handle your fine art pieces, antiques, and other valuable items with special care.

How long does white-glove delivery usually take?

The duration of the move will depend on the distance to be traveled and the volume of your possessions. The moving process may take from 1-2 days for a local move and between 4-21 days for a long-distance cross-country move.

How to prepare for a white-glove move?

First of all, pack your personal items, wallet, credit cards, IDs, documents, chargers, valuable items, etc. Clean your home and clear pathways inside your place – remove stuff that might prevent movers from carrying your boxes. Then just watch the process, as our movers will take care of the rest.

How early do I need to schedule my moving date?

You should schedule your move date as early as you can (ссылка на блог How long in advance should I book movers). If you’re moving locally, at least 1-2 weeks in advance, for long-distance moves, we recommend getting a quote at least 2-4 weeks before your move date.

How much does white-glove moving cost?

Your white glove moving cost depends on factors, such as the type and size of your property, the complexity of your move, the number of bedrooms, the volume of your items, zip code, etc. At SOS Moving, we offer flat and hourly rates. The hourly rate depends on the number of movers to be involved and the size of the moving truck. Flat rate is based on the volume of belongings you need to move, and it’s the best option for long-distance moves. To know your moving cost, please contact us or submit a form on our website to request your free quote.

Will packers and movers pack everything?

Yes, we can pack everything at your home or office except prohibited items, valuables, and personal items. Prohibited items include perishables, hazardous materials, firearms, batteries, etc.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major debit and credit cards, and certified checks. We don’t accept personal checks. We do not charge any transaction fees on card transactions.
