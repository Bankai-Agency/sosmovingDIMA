What to Do With Leftover Moving Boxes?

What to Do With Leftover Moving Boxes?

Last Updated: 
Friday, October 16, 2020

Table of Contents

    If you already unpacked in your new home and want to finally enjoy some free time, there might be another task you need to handle. After the unpacking process is over, there will be some leftover moving boxes in your home. Besides getting rid of them, there are other ways you can put these packing supplies to good use. Take a look at some of the best ideas for using the leftover moving boxes after the relocation day is over.

    Why are moving boxes the best solution when packing for the move?

    During the moving process, finding packing supplies can take a lot of time. Besides having to pack different-shaped items, people usually underestimate the number of supplies they’ll need. For this reason, getting cardboard moving boxes can be the best solution, since these supplies are cheap and easy to find. No matter how many items you need to relocate, some sturdy moving boxes can ease your moving day. Moving boxes are versatile, easy to handle, and cheap to get. The best part about using this type of supplies is that you can use them later on. Whether you want to use them for your next move, make them into storage boxes, or something else, leftover moving boxes are something you shouldn’t toss away.

    In case you are just getting ready for the move, you should contact a reliable moving company like SOS Moving and Storage Los Angeles. Besides providing packing supplies, an experienced moving company can help you with packing and transportation services. Make sure to let your movers know more details of your moving plans.

    What can you do with leftover moving boxes?

    The use of leftover moving boxes is very versatile. Since these boxes are easy to fold, carry, and assemble, there are many options you have. Here are some of the examples of what you can do with the moving boxes you have left after the move:

    • Use them for your next move. If your cardboard boxes are still in good shape, there is no need to toss them away.
    • Make a home-storage from the leftover moving boxes. If you lack room in your home, storing belongings in these boxes can be the best idea.
    • Use the moving boxes for decoration, DIY crafts, or fun activities with children. Cardboard is a very versatile material, so you can use it for many purposes. You can easily cut, color, and reshape it so it becomes a nice decorative item.

    Preserving boxes for the next move

    Since this is the most common use of the leftover moving boxes, you should know how to properly do it. What is tricky about cardboard boxes is that they are sensitive to moisture and dirt. In case they get damaged during relocation, there is no smart way to preserve them. This is why you should use only the best quality, sturdy boxes when planning a relocation. In order to have a successful moving day, you should try to avoid any mishaps with packing supplies like breakage of the boxes. In case you plan to use the leftover moving boxes for another relocation, inspect them first and find out if they are still in good shape.

    Using leftover moving boxes for storage

    Not every home has perfect-sized storage. If you need to store your belongings safely at home or in a rental unit, you can use leftover moving boxes for this task. For an in-home storage solution, you can stack smaller boxes on top of the bigger ones. Another thing you can do is decorate the boxes if you plan to display them in your home. Keep in mind that not every storage solution has to be hidden in your home, so these boxes can become a part of the decorations.

    Another use of the leftover moving boxes is for keeping belongings safe inside a storage unit. If you find a quality storage facility which is dry and has good air circulation, you can pack your belongings into cardboard boxes safely. For more tips on how to pack for storage, you can always find some packing tips online. Most moving tips and tricks you can read online will help you find ideas for making a home storage space.

Besides saving time, the best thing about hiring professional packing services in LA is that you can get the best quality packing supplies. Hence, quality moving boxes you use for your relocation can be used after the moving day is over. Let your movers know more details of the size and weight of your inventory and they will suggest the right number of boxes.

    Moving soon? Here’s where you can find packaging boxes

    Before you decide to reuse the leftover moving boxes, you should know where you can get them in the first place. One of the best ways to find them without losing too much time is to contact a local moving company. Most movers in the US provide different packaging supplies, but also offer reliable packing services. In case you are moving on a tight budget, you can always consider visiting a local market, a book store, or an office space and ask if they have some leftover moving boxes to give. These packing materials are versatile, sturdy, and cheap to get. No matter how big your relocation is, you can always preserve some of them and put them to good use.
