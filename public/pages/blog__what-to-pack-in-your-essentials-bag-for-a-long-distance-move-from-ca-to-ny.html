What to Pack In Your Essentials Bag for a Long Distance Move From CA to NY?

What to Pack In Your Essentials Bag for a Long Distance Move From CA to NY?

Last Updated: 
Monday, June 7, 2021

Table of Contents

    Do you need to move your entire household from California to New York? Then, you need to organize your relocation. Since this is a long-distance move, you cannot easily go back to your old house if you forget something once you move. In addition to this, if you are moving into an empty house, you will not have an access to your items until they get delivered by the best moving companies Los Angeles. You need to survive and feel comfortable in your new house for the first couple of days or even weeks after relocation. For this reason, you need to prepare one or several essential bags for your relocation. What do you need to pack in your essentials bag for a long distance move from CA to NY? Here are all the most common things.

    Clothes to pack in your essentials bag for a long distance move from CA to NY

    First of all, you will need to pack a change of clothes in your essential bag. How to do this? If you decide to pack your items on your own, then this is going to be quite easy. Once you start packing your closet, put aside your most comfortable pieces of clothing. Later, you can put these clothes in your essential bag and bring them with you to your new home. This way, you will have all the necessary pieces of clothing. On the other hand, if you will hire packing services Los Angeles to pack and move your household, then you need to put aside clothes you want to bring with you a couple of days before movers come to your house. Do not forget to do this since movers will most likely pack everything without asking.

    Electronic devices and their charges

    The next thing you should put into your essential bag would be all your electronic devices and their charges. Since it is almost impossible to live in today’s world without technology, you should keep it close to yourself. In addition to this, you should not leave your expensive computers, laptops, and other devices to be moved and transported with the rest of your household items. After all, if something happens to your equipment, it is going to be pretty expensive to replace it or to repair it. For this reason, you should be the one packing and moving your electronic devices. As for the rest, you should hire Los Angeles interstate movers and let them take care of everything. Furthermore, you should be in contact with your movers, new landlord, family, and friends during the relocation process. This is why you need to bring your phone and computer.

    Toiletries to pack in your essentials bag for a long distance move from CA to NY

    Since you are going to be traveling for a while, you also need to bring basic toiletries with you. Depending on your needs, age, and everything else, you should prepare the following.

    • A small shampoo – even though you can buy it anywhere, it is always better to have a small bottle ready just in case. After a long journey, there is nothing more satisfying and relaxing than taking a shower.
    • Toilet paper – again, just in case if there isn’t any available.
    • A toothbrush and a toothpaste – do not forget to pack your toothbrush and your toothpaste for your travel.
    • Face creams – Again, it depends if you use any face creams for your skin. If you do, then, do not forget to bring them with you.
    • A small hand sanitizer – in case you need to clean and disinfect your hands during your journey.

    Medicine

    Next on the list would be medicine. This of course depends on whether you take certain medications. If you do, then you should visit your doctor before your relocation. Make sure you have enough medicine for the first couple of weeks in your new house. It will take some time until you find a new doctor and till you can get your new subscription. In addition to this, when you meet your doctor, you should also ask for some medicine that can help you if you feel nauseous, dizzy, develop a headache during your travel. Sometimes, organizing an entire relocation, even if you have help from movers California to New York, can be extremely mentally and physically exhausting. For this reason, it is extremely important to take care of your health.

    Documents pack in your essentials bag for a long distance move from CA to NY

    There are certain documents that you should always keep next to you and not with moving companies Glendale CA. These documents would include your birth certificate, your passport, your driver’s license, your marriage certificate, your mortgage, bank documents, and so on. For this reason, you should also buy one binder or a folder and keep all of your important documents here. Then, you should put this folder in your essential bag just in case you need them. In addition to this, it can be quite inconvenient if you happen to lose any of these important documents. In order to prevent this, you should make a couple of copies of your documents and have them notarized. This way, even if something happens, and you’ll always have enough copies for you to use.

    Food and toys

    The last set of items that you should pack in your essentials bag a long distance move from CA to NY would be food and toys. First of all, you need to pack food, especially if your fridge in your new house is empty. In order not to rush around with delivery or preparing food once you arrive at your new house, have something super prepared and packed for your travels. On the other hand, if you have children, you should also pack toys for them. This way, you will keep them entertained during your travel. They will also feel less agitated once you all arrive in your new house.
