    Life in Los Angeles is surely quite exciting. This city is one of the most popular cities in the entire world because of the booming entertainment industry, hot weather, plenty of beaches, and entertaining lifestyle. For this reason, many people would like to live in this city. If you’re one of them, then you might plan to buy your first house in Los Angeles. As you might know, buying a house in any city is quite challenging, especially for first-timers. In order to successfully move to this city with movers Los Angeles, here are the best LA suburbs for first-time home buyers. Most of these have suburbs have houses listed for the price between $700,000 and $1,000,000 with two to three bedrooms and around 1000 square feet on average.

    Valley Village, Burbank, and North Hollywood as LA suburbs for first-time home buyers

    The first suburb you should consider would be the Valley. It has one of the best hiking trails around, amazing restaurants, and even moving studios. The median home price is around $900,000 but the houses are great for first-time buyers. Then, you should consider buying a house in Burbank. You can find Warner Brothers and Walt Disney studios here in this neighborhood. Considering the popularity of these two studios, the house prices are a little bit all over then in Valley village. On average, you can find out a good house for $770,000. In addition to this, you should also take a look at North Hollywood. The median home value is around $650,000 and it is the most affordable suburb in Los Angeles. Find your new LA suburb before hiring moving companies Pasadena to help you with relocation.

    Valley Glen and Highland Park

    Another good suburb you should consider would be Valley Glen. This is one of the more diverse areas in LA. It is home to the Great Wall of Los Angeles which tells a story through the eyes of minorities. The median house price would be around $700,000 which makes this neighborhood one of the more affordable ones. This is a good option for people who and do not have a large budget and also have to book Los Angeles apartment movers. In addition to this, you should also consider moving to Highland Park. This is one of the oldest areas in Los Angeles. Furthermore, this is one of the most artistic suburbs in the entire city. If you feel like you belong to this scene, then you should buy your first home here. Luckily, the average price for a house is also around $700,000.

    Montecito Heights and Atwater Village as LA suburbs for first-time home buyers

    Probably the cheapest suburb in the northeast Los Angeles area would be Montecito Heights. The median house price for this neighborhood would be $590,000. Many people would believe that there is something wrong with this neighborhood because the home prices are quite low considering it is still Los Angeles. However, this suburb is one of the most peaceful places and perfect when moving with kids. In addition to this, it has plenty of green space and perfect for playing sports outside. If you happen to prefer a more vibrant area, then you should consider buying a house in Atwater Village. The average home price is a little bit less than $800,000. Still, if you prefer to live in a lively neighborhood, then this one would be perfect for you. Make sure to organize your moving budget accordingly so you will have enough money for everything.

    Mount Washington, Crenshaw Manor, and Leimert Park

    Mount Washington is yet another peaceful neighborhood that is separated from downtown Los Angeles with Hills. It is a quiet residential area perfect for raising a family. If you want to avoid stress caused by living in a big city, then this neighborhood is perfect for you. The median house price is around $790,000. Then, Crenshaw Manor has recently been extremely popular among first-time homebuyers. This is a quite small neighborhood where the median listing price is around $740,000. It is another quiet residential area which is the reason behind this recent popularity. In addition to this, you should also consider moving to Leimert Park. This is the area of the historic Leimert Park hence the name of the neighborhood. you can find a good house for at least $750,000. This area is also oriented towards families.

    View Park – Windsor Hills and Mid-City as LA suburbs for first-time home buyers

    If you want to live in View Park – Windsor Hills, then prepare to buy a house for at least $830,000. This area is also known to be one of the wealthiest African American communities. In addition to this, this area has been going through development so home prices might increase in the near future. Then, you can also consider Mid-city as your new home. This part of Los Angeles is ideal for first-time homebuyers. It has all the necessary places one needs to have in their neighborhood. In addition to this, this suburb is close to Venice Boulevard, one of the most popular attractions nearby. If you plan to buy a house here, the home prices range from $500,000 to $2.4 million. In order to buy the best house for you, you should consider hiring a real state agency.

    More places to consider

    Los Angeles has plenty of places you should consider for your future home. For this reason, here are more LA suburbs for first-time homebuyers.

    • Eagle Rock – a family-friendly neighborhoodwith an average home price of $650,000
    • Marina del Rey – a fun suburb, popular among tourists with an average home price of $800,000
    • Ladera Heights – with thriving tech scene, and the average home price of $1,1 million
