    Perfect weather and beaches

    The best time of year to visit Los Angeles is from late May to mid-October, with a peak score in the first week of July.  Weather is almost perfect with around 300 sunny days a year and average temperatures from 48°F to 85°F. Venice Beach is a highly popular attraction for local and international tourists alike. It has got a 2.5-mile Ocean Front Walk for pedestrians only and hosts various street artists. Other famous beaches equally attractive but unique are Malibu,  Manhattan Beach, Santa Monica Beach, El Matador, Leo Carrillo State Park, and many more.
