    It is quite easy to stay in touch with your friends and family if you are moving locally. If you can see your family almost every single day or you do not have to spend more than a couple of hours driving to see them, then, you will have no problems keeping in touch. However, if you will across the country, living thousands and thousands of miles apart can be a problem. Due to the distance, different time zones, and everyday life, you might find it hard to call your friends and family. For this reason, here are all the tips for staying in touch after a long distance move to NYC from CA with Southern California movers.

    Staying in touch after a long distance move to NYC from CA by calling them

    It is quite easy to make a phone call. You need to make a phone call when you’re searching for appliance movers Los Angeles. Then, you need to make phone calls in order to organize your entire relocation. Basically, you are making phone calls every single day. One of these could be you still keep in touch with your family and friends. Since almost everyone has a cell phone, it is quite easy to call your family friends almost every single day. The only problem would be that everyone is quite busy with their own lives. However, you should put an effort into calling them frequently. This does not have to be every single day, but at least two or three times per week would be perfect. Also, you need to figure out the time difference as well. Try to call when both of you are not busy.

    Use social media

    Almost everyone has a profile on social media. You are probably one of them as well. For this reason, you should use your social media accounts in order to keep in touch with your family and friends. This is actually easier than making phone calls. By now, you know how all the social media function, you make a profile, follow or befriend people, and you can see their pictures and posts. You can see what your friends and family have been up to just by looking at their photos. As you might know, many people like to overshare on their social media. Therefore, it is quite easy to see and keep in touch just by liking or commenting on one of their posts. In addition to this, you can even find movers California to New York using social media.

    Staying in touch after a long distance move to NYC from CA by sharing photos

    Even though having a social media account is quite popular, not everyone likes to post or share pictures from their lives. In addition to this, some private pictures remain private. For this reason, you should not forget to share photos with your friends and family while you live across the country. You can use your social media for this since you can send private messages to your friends and family. It is always pretty exciting to receive a photo from your loved ones. Since you cannot be there with them, you can stay updated by sharing photos of each other. Apart from this, you can also share photos while organizing your move or when traveling to your new home. This way, your family will know how the entire process of moving is going on, how new your house looks like, and so on.

    Send each other presents or care packages

    The other great way to keep in touch would be to send each other presents or care packages. When you are living so far away from each other, the best way to show that you are still thinking about your friends and family would be to send them a small present. This does not have to be anything big or flashy. Just think about something that your friends and family would like to have. The best present would be something that would remind your friends and family of you. In addition to this, it would be better if you do not tell your friends or family that you are sending them a present so it would be a surprise for them when they receive it. If you do not have any ideas, you can always look up the best present ideas on the Internet.

    Staying in touch after a long distance move to NYC from CA by planning a trip together

    It is hard to live far away from your family. Therefore, you should try to think about a way to see them again. This is where organizing or planning a trip together comes in. When you are organizing a trip together with your family or friends, it will keep you occupied and excited. you can either visit each other in New York City or California or you can meet in the middle. There are plenty of beautiful cities between New York and California. You can choose a city where you have never been before. So, you can explore a new city, meet with your friends and family, and have a good time. As you can see, this would be a perfect opportunity to explore other cities in the area. You can also welcome your friends and family to your new house and show them around your new city.

    All the tips and tricks

    As you can see, staying in touch after a long distance move to NYC from CA should not be hard or tiresome. To recap, here are all the ways to stay in touch.

    • Making phone calls
    • Using social media
    • Sending pictures
    • Sending presents
    • Planning a trip

    However, in order to successfully maintain a good relationship, you need to make an effort. Even if you are busy or tired, make sure to send a message or make a video call to your family and friends.
