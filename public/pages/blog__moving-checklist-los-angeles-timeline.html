Ultimate Moving Checklist for Los Angeles: Complete Timeline Guide

Ultimate Moving Checklist for Los Angeles: Complete Timeline Guide

Last Updated: 
Saturday, November 1, 2025

Table of Contents

    Moving involves hundreds of tasks that interconnect in ways you'll only discover through mistakes—unless you have a comprehensive checklist. Los Angeles adds unique requirements like parking permits, HOA notifications, earthquake kit transfers, and beach community restrictions that generic checklists miss. This timeline-based checklist, developed from SOS Moving's thousands of LA relocations, ensures nothing gets forgotten while maintaining your sanity through the process.

    8 Weeks Before Moving Day

    Research and planning phase:☐ Create moving binder/digital folder for documents☐ Research moving companies, get 3-5 quotes☐ Check peak seasons (May-Sept higher prices)☐ Verify mover licenses at CPUC.ca.gov☐ Read reviews across multiple platforms☐ Set moving budget with 20% buffer☐ Take photos of valuable items☐ Start decluttering room by room

    LA-specific tasks:☐ Check new area parking restrictions☐ Research HOA rules if applicable☐ Verify building moving hours/requirements☐ Check street cleaning schedules both locations☐ Note any film shoot notifications

    6 Weeks Before Moving

    Booking and documentation:☐ Book moving company with deposit☐ Schedule time off work☐ Order moving supplies if DIY packing☐ Create home inventory spreadsheet☐ Photograph electronics setups☐ Begin using frozen food items☐ Research new neighborhood services☐ Start collecting important documents

    School-related (if applicable):☐ Notify current school of move☐ Request records transfer☐ Research new schools/districts☐ Check enrollment requirements☐ Update emergency contacts

    Financial tasks:☐ Notify mortgage/rental companies☐ Check lease-breaking penalties☐ Document security deposit condition☐ Budget for deposits/connections☐ Update employer if needed

    5 Weeks Before Moving

    Paperwork and services:☐ Submit change of address (USPS.com)☐ Update driver's license plans☐ Transfer voter registration☐ Update vehicle registration plans☐ Notify insurance companies (all types)☐ Contact utility companies both locations☐ Schedule cable/internet disconnection☐ Arrange new service installations

    Medical preparations:☐ Request medical records☐ Get prescription refills☐ Research new doctors/dentists☐ Transfer veterinary records☐ Update pet microchips☐ Get vaccine records

    4 Weeks Before Moving

    Active preparation phase:☐ Begin packing non-essentials☐ Label boxes by room and contents☐ Use up cleaning supplies☐ Donate unused items☐ Sell valuable unwanted items☐ Arrange charity pickups☐ Back up computer files☐ Update subscriptions addresses

    LA-specific preparations:☐ Reserve parking for moving day☐ Get parking permits if required☐ Schedule elevator (high-rises)☐ Notify neighbors of moving date☐ Check earthquake kit contents☐ Plan for pool/hot tub if applicable

    Important notifications:☐ Banks and credit cards☐ Employer HR department☐ Subscription services☐ Gym memberships☐ Professional licenses☐ Alumni associations

    3 Weeks Before Moving

    Intensify packing efforts:☐ Pack seasonal items☐ Pack decorations and artwork☐ Wrap fragile items carefully☐ Pack books (small boxes)☐ Create "Open First" box☐ Pack garage/storage areas☐ Photograph box contents☐ Number boxes sequentially

    Service confirmations:☐ Confirm moving company details☐ Verify truck size adequate☐ Confirm arrival time☐ Review contract terms☐ Understand payment methods☐ Arrange cash for tips

    Vehicle preparations:☐ Service car if driving distance☐ Check registration currency☐ Clean out car completely☐ Plan route if driving☐ Arrange vehicle shipping if needed

    2 Weeks Before Moving

    Final preparations:☐ Pack everything except essentials☐ Defrost and clean refrigerator☐ Clean oven and appliances☐ Confirm travel arrangements☐ Arrange pet transportation☐ Get cash for moving day☐ Pack suitcase for moving week☐ Create inventory list

    Children's preparations:☐ Pack special comfort items accessible☐ Prepare entertainment for moving day☐ Discuss move positively☐ Say goodbye to friends☐ Pack school supplies separately

    Final arrangements:☐ Confirm parking permits posted☐ Double-check elevator reservation☐ Verify building requirements met☐ Confirm utility disconnections☐ Schedule final walk-through☐ Arrange cleaning service

    1 Week Before Moving

    Final week tasks:☐ Pack daily-use items☐ Clean as you empty rooms☐ Do final laundry☐ Empty safety deposit box☐ Return borrowed items☐ Collect loaned items☐ Dispose hazardous materials☐ Pack cleaning supplies last

    Electronics and valuables:☐ Backup phones and tablets☐ Pack chargers accessibly☐ Secure jewelry and documents☐ Pack medications separately☐ Remove batteries from remotes☐ Pack electronics in original boxes

    Final confirmations:☐ Call moving company to confirm☐ Verify payment method ready☐ Check weather forecast☐ Plan moving day meals☐ Arrange child/pet care☐ Set multiple alarms

    48 Hours Before Moving

    Almost there:☐ Pack remaining items☐ Clean out all closets☐ Check all storage areas☐ Empty bathroom cabinets☐ Pack shower items☐ Prepare payment for movers☐ Charge all devices☐ Get good night's sleep

    Final food tasks:☐ Empty refrigerator completely☐ Dispose of perishables☐ Pack cooler if needed☐ Plan meals for moving day☐ Pack snacks and water

    Moving Day

    Before movers arrive:☐ Strip beds, pack linens☐ Do final bathroom check☐ Protect floors if raining☐ Clear pathways completely☐ Secure parking spaces☐ Have inventory list ready

    During loading:☐ Be present and available☐ Check inventory carefully☐ Document any damage☐ Keep important items separate☐ Stay hydrated☐ Direct special handling items

    Before leaving:☐ Check every room, closet, cabinet☐ Check garage and storage☐ Turn off all utilities☐ Lock all windows☐ Leave keys and remotes☐ Take final meter readings☐ Leave forwarding information

    At new home:☐ Be present before movers☐ Protect new floors☐ Direct box placement☐ Check inventory arrival☐ Note any damage immediately☐ Set up beds first☐ Locate essentials box

    First Week After Moving

    Immediate priorities:☐ Register children for school☐ Update driver's license (10 days)☐ Register to vote (30 days)☐ Update vehicle registration (20 days)☐ Find new doctors/dentist☐ Update employer records☐ Explore neighborhood

    Home setup:☐ Test all utilities☐ Check appliances function☐ Locate circuit breakers☐ Find water shut-offs☐ Test smoke detectors☐ Set up earthquake kit☐ Meet neighbors

    Administrative tasks:☐ Update insurance policies☐ Register with new city☐ Get library card☐ Update professional licenses☐ Join local groups☐ Review HOA rules

    Los Angeles-Specific Reminders

    Parking and permits:LA parking enforcement is aggressive. Permits required for moving trucks. Street cleaning tickets are $73. Beach communities have special restrictions. Downtown requires building coordination.

    Utility considerations:LADWP requires deposits. SoCal Gas separate service. Spectrum vs Frontier availability varies. Solar panels need transfer coordination. Pool service arrangements.

    Emergency preparedness:Earthquake kit essential. Wildfire evacuation plans. Tsunami zones (beach areas). Power outage supplies. Water storage recommended.
