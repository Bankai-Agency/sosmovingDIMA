Frequently Asked Questions

Do you pack and move house plants?

We recommend keeping your plants with you during the move or using shipping services. Moving companies cannot ensure that they’ll be transported safely, especially when you’re moving long-distance, so it’s better to make other arrangements.

What are the items I can not pack in a moving truck?

A safe relocation is our goal, so we’ll provide you with a detailed list of items that are prohibited to transport. It includes perishables, hazardous materials, firearms, batteries, etc. We also don’t recommend packing your personal and irreplaceable items, including family heirlooms, family photos, jewelry, documents, etc. Carry them with you during the move.

What does full packing service mean?

Our full packing service means that we’ll pack all your possessions for you, and you won’t have to lift a finger. We’ll bring boxes, bubble wrap, and all packing materials and pack your stuff a couple of days prior to your move day.

Where can I get moving boxes and packing paper? Do you provide packing materials?

Sure, our movers can bring packing materials and boxes if you need them. Make sure you mention that you need them to our operators in advance.

Can I do my own packing?

Sure, you can pack yourself, but keep in mind that in this case your possessions will not be covered by our moving insurance. We recommend leaving the packing job to our professional team, especially when you’re moving for longer distances, so we can guarantee proper packing and safe delivery.

Will packers and movers pack everything?

Yes, we can pack everything at your home or office except prohibited items, valuables, and personal items. Prohibited items include perishables, hazardous materials, firearms, batteries, etc.

How do I pack my valuable items and jewelry?

You should pack and carry your valuable items, important papers, passports, credit cards, jewelry, etc. with you. Don’t pack them into a moving truck with other boxes.

Do you offer customized packing services?

Yes, we do. Ask our operators or your moving coordinator what services you want to be included in your moving estimate. We provide full-service packing, partial packing, and unpacking services.
