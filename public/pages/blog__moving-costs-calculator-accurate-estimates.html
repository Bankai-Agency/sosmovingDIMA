Moving Costs Calculator - Accurate Estimates

Last Updated: 
Saturday, December 6, 2025

Table of Contents

    Online moving calculators promise instant estimates but deliver fantasy numbers. Enter "2-bedroom apartment" and get quotes from $200 to $2,000—neither accurate. Real moving costs depend on dozens of factors these calculators ignore. Here's how to calculate your actual LA moving costs using SOS Moving's transparent pricing and real-world variables.

    The Basic Formula

    Moving Cost = (Hourly Rate × Actual Hours) + Fixed Fees + Variables

    Simple formula, complex variables. Let's break down each component.

    Step 1: Determine Crew Size

    2 movers ($119-145/hour):

    • Studio apartments
    • 1-bedroom (no stairs)
    • Minimal furniture
    • You're helping

    3 movers ($159-185/hour):

    • Standard 2-bedroom
    • Normal furniture amount
    • Some stairs
    • Average difficulty

    4 movers ($199-225/hour):

    • 3+ bedrooms
    • Heavy furniture
    • Multiple stairs
    • Time constraints
    • Luxury items

    Crew size calculator:

    • Base: 2 movers
    • Add 1 for: Each bedroom over 1
    • Add 1 for: Significant stairs
    • Add 1 for: Heavy items (piano, safe)

    Step 2: Calculate Realistic Hours

    Base hours by home size:

    • Studio: 2-3 hours
    • 1-bedroom: 3-4 hours
    • 2-bedroom: 4-6 hours
    • 3-bedroom: 6-8 hours
    • 4-bedroom: 8-10 hours

    Add time for:

    • Each flight of stairs: +30 minutes
    • Long carry (50+ feet): +30 minutes
    • Elevator wait: +20 minutes
    • Packing help: +1-2 hours
    • Rush hour traffic: +45 minutes
    • Difficult parking: +30 minutes

    Subtract time for:

    • Everything packed/ready: -30 minutes
    • Ground floor both ends: -30 minutes
    • Minimal belongings: -1 hour

    Step 3: Day and Time Multipliers

    SOS Moving rates:

    Monday-Thursday:

    • 2 movers: $119 (cash) / $129 (card)
    • 3 movers: $159 (cash) / $169 (card)
    • 4 movers: $199 (cash) / $209 (card)

    Friday-Sunday:

    • 2 movers: $135 (cash) / $145 (card)
    • 3 movers: $175 (cash) / $185 (card)
    • 4 movers: $215 (cash) / $225 (card)

    Time factors:

    • End of month: Add 20%
    • Holiday weeks: Add 25%
    • December 31st: Add 40%
    • Summer peak: Add 15%

    Step 4: Fixed Fees

    Always included:

    • Gas fee: $30
    • Minimum hours: 3 (or 4 if 30+ miles)
    • Basic insurance: Included

    Potential additions:

    • Piano: $200
    • Extra heavy items (300+ lbs): $150
    • Overnight hold: $200
    • COI processing: $50
    • Deposit (applied to total): $50

    Step 5: Distance Calculations

    Local moves (under 10 miles):Standard rates apply

    Medium distance (10-30 miles):Double drive time applies (CPUC rule)Add 1 hour to calculation

    Long distance (30+ miles):4-hour minimumDouble drive timeConsider traffic patterns

    Distance time calculator:

    • West LA to Downtown: +45 minutes
    • Valley to South Bay: +1 hour
    • LA to Orange County: +1.5 hours

    Factors That Break Calculators

    Standard calculators assume:

    • Everything fits categories
    • No special circumstances
    • Perfect conditions
    • Average everything

    Reality includes:

    • Hoarder situations
    • Surprise pianos
    • Building restrictions
    • Weather issues
    • Traffic variations
    • Parking problems

    Real estimates require human evaluation.

    Quick Estimate Formula

    For rough planning:

    (Number of bedrooms × $250) + $350 base

    • Studio: $350-450
    • 1-bedroom: $600-750
    • 2-bedroom: $850-1,000
    • 3-bedroom: $1,100-1,400

    5. No traffic consideration
    6. Optimistic packing timeline
    7. Ignoring distance rules
