Frequently Asked Questions

How much do movers cost in Los Angeles?

Our rates start at $119 per hour for 2 movers and a truck on weekdays and $135 per hour on weekends. A typical studio or one-bedroom move takes 3 to 5 hours. The minimum booking time is 3 hours. All protective materials — blankets, shrink wrap, tape, wardrobe boxes — are included at no extra cost.

What is included in your moving service?

Every move includes premium moving blankets, unlimited shrink wrap, heavy-duty packing tape, wardrobe boxes, furniture disassembly and reassembly, TV unmounting, stair carries, long carries, and multiple stops. We never charge extra for these — they are part of our standard service.

Do you offer same-day moving services?

Yes, we offer same-day and next-day moving services across Los Angeles, subject to crew availability. For guaranteed scheduling, we recommend booking 1 to 2 weeks in advance for local moves and 2 to 4 weeks for long-distance relocations.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of Los Angeles County including Beverly Hills, Santa Monica, Pasadena, Glendale, Burbank, Long Beach, Hollywood, the San Fernando Valley, and the South Bay. We also cover Orange County and provide long-distance moving to any state in the U.S.

How do I get a free moving estimate?

Call us at 909-443-0004 or fill out the form on our free estimate page. We will assess your inventory, distance, and any special requirements and provide a detailed quote — usually within 15 minutes.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. SOS Moving is fully licensed by the California Public Utilities Commission and registered with USDOT (3398018). We carry comprehensive liability insurance on every move.

What is Double Drive Time?

Per CPUC regulations, moves where the distance between origin and destination exceeds 10 miles are subject to a Double Drive Time charge. This means the driving time between locations is counted twice in your billing. We always explain this before booking.
