    1. Call police (they'll make landlord open)
    2. Call us immediately
    3. We'll arrive with police
    4. Move everything out
    5. Document for lawsuit

    You have rights:

    • Immediate re-entry
    • Damages recoverable
    • Moving costs reimbursable
    • Hotel costs covered
    • Sue for wrongful eviction

    We'll testify if needed.
