How to Pack a Fine China Collection?

How to Pack a Fine China Collection?

Last Updated: 
Sunday, August 29, 2021

Table of Contents

    Do you like to collect fine China? Do you have a big collection? Then, it means that you treasure it dearly and make sure those pieces are in good condition. However, what happens if you need to move? Maybe you found a beautiful house where you can display your China collection the way you always wanted. Furthermore, maybe you are moving because you got an amazing job opportunity, need a change of scenario, or moving in with your partner. Whatever the case might be, you are moving. This means organizing everything, from booking SOS Moving LA movers to packing everything that you own, including your China collection. Because of its specifics, here is how you should pack a fine China collection for moving.

    Hire a moving company before you pack a fine China collection

    First of all, you should definitely hire a moving company if you plan to relocate anytime soon. It is quite difficult to manage your everyday obligations with preparing for your relocation. The reason behind this would be the time and energy you need to dedicate to properly prepare your relocation. On the other hand, if you look for movers Palmdale CA, you will find out soon it is going to be so much easier to handle the entire relocation. The same applies to your fine China collection. You need to be extremely careful since usually fine China is quite fragile. If you do not hire reliable movers and try to do everything by yourself, there is a high possibility of damaging something. However, if you hire movers, they will make sure that everything is safe and secure. If not, get insurance so you can claim it later if necessary.

    Declutter your collection

    This might be hard, but you need to declutter your collection before packing it. The reason is quite simple. Do you really need to move everything that you own? This does not only mean a fine China collection but also your entire household. However, regarding your collection, you need to make sure that every single piece is in good condition. If some of your China pieces are old, broken, chipped at the site, are sustained any other damage, you should decide whether to move them or not. If those pieces are not valuable or they do not hold any sentimental value, maybe it is time to say goodbye to them. Why is this important? If you decide to use packing services Los Angeles then you will pay less if movers have fewer items to pack. Furthermore, it is also going to be easier for you to pack too.

    Gather all the necessary packing supplies to pack a fine China collection

    Once you have the number of items you want to move, it is time to gather all the necessary packing supplies. Otherwise, you will have nothing to pack your fine China collection with. What do you need? The usual packing supplies would include the following.

    • Cardboard boxes
    • Bubble wrapping
    • Packing peanuts
    • Tape

    Generally speaking, you can use newspapers to secure your fine China. However, newspapers do not offer the necessary protection and they can leave stains. For this reason, it is better to use bubble wrapping in order to protect your items. You will need a lot of bubble wrapping. You can get all of these supplies from long distance movers Los Angeles or by checking online. If you plan to collect all the packing supplies on your own, make sure that boxes are in good condition. They need to protect your items.

    Wash and clean your collection properly

    If your fine China collection is pretty big, it means that you are not probably using every single piece daily. For this reason, you need to properly clean everything before you decide to pack them. You should never pack items that are dusty, or dirty and this also includes your China. By now, you should already know how to properly clean your fine China collection. If you decide just to wipe the dust, make sure to do it early. On the other hand, if you decide to wash it with water and soap, make sure they are properly dry before putting them inside the box. You should never pack items that are slightly wet. Everything should be completely dry.

    If not, then mold can develop on your items and cause huge damage not only to the item on it but also to everything else.  

    Steps to take when you need to pack a fine China collection

    Now that you have everything ready and prepared, it is time to actually pack your China pieces. Start by using bubble wrapping. Make sure you have enough because you need to wrap every single item that you own. You should also do it separately. This way, you will ensure that all the pieces will be well protected and safe during transport. Once you use bubble wrapping, you need to safely secure it with the tape. Then, put all the wrapped pieces inside the box is. Make sure not to overstuff the box so it will not break during transport. In addition to this, you need to fill the empty space inside the box so your wrapped pieces will not be able to move. You can use packing peanuts for this purpose. After everything, make sure to label the box so everyone will know that fine China is inside.

    It is not difficult to pack it

    As you can see, when you need to pack a fine China collection, it is not that hard. Actually, packing your fine China is more time-consuming than difficult. Since you need to separately bubble wrap all the pieces, it can be a little bit tiresome. But, this way, every single piece will reach your new address in perfect condition. For this reason, make sure to get enough packing supplies so you will be able to properly pack and transport everything. Do not forget to hire a moving company for additional help.
