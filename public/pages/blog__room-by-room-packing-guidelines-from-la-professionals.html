Room-By-Room Packing Guidelines From LA Professionals

Room-By-Room Packing Guidelines From LA Professionals

Last Updated: 
Monday, October 5, 2020

Table of Contents

    Packing your home room by room is one of the best and easiest ways to take care of the packing process. It’s not easy, and it’s not really fun, but packing is a huge part of your relocation. This is why you need to make sure you are doing a great job when it comes to it. Room-by-room packing allows you to be organized and to keep track of your progress with ease. Once you are done with this part of the move, your reliable LA movers can relocate everything to your new home. But first, you need to get to know just how room by room packing works. Doing it like a pro is easy if you get advice from LA professionals as well!

    Why is this a good way to pack your home for relocation?

    Room-by-room packing is an easy and fast way to pack your home. This way, the unpacking process won’t be a problem at all. So, you need to make sure you know how to do it right. This way, you won’t have to worry about finding anything that you need at any point in time. Packing room by room is great not only because you can see how many jobs you have left to do. But because you will be more efficient while packing, and you will easily realize how many moving boxes you need for each room. So, if you decided that you want to move to Los Angeles, and you don’t want to get a packing service for your relocation, you should definitely choose to pack room-by-room. And, if you choose to get a packing service, everything will go much smoother!

    How do you pack room-by-room?

    Now that you understand that room-by-room packing will give you the ability to take over control over this entire event, you need to know just how to conduct it. It’s not hard, but you need to realize just how easy it can be if you organize the right way. Here is how you do packing by rooms in no time:

    • You need to ask people for help
    • Get the right packing supplies
    • Pack room by room, but really
    • Label every single box
    • Colorcode

    Knowing how to do this right will surely make it much easier for you, so don’t worry, just start and you will do just fine!

    Get some help!

    You need to get some helping hands on this project with you. Ask your friends and relatives to help you pack, and this way, you will avoid getting a packing service as well. The only way for you to have a successful relocation is by hiring professional long-distance movers, and let them move your belongings, so this is another form of help you will need. Once you have all the help you need around you, you can get to work!

    Once your relocation is done, you can throw a goodbye party for your helpers! You will be able to relax after a great job.

    Get the packing supplies for your room-by-room packing

    You will need the packing supplies, of course. But, if you are planning to pack a room-by-room, you will easily know how many boxes you need. It’s easier to conclude how many boxes you need to pack one room than to pack an entire home, so this kind of divided packing space is a great idea. If you need to pack fragile items, you need to remember to get separate moving boxes for those. This way, they won’t get damaged in the moving process.

    Pack room-by-room

    Now that you have your helpers and supplies, you can start packing. The point of packing room by room is to pack one room at a time. So, if you started packing your bedroom, you can pack all the things from there in the moving boxes that are just for that. If you have some pillows that you keep and use only in your bedroom, you shouldn’t pack them in the same box as the pillows you have on your living room couch. This way, you will be more efficient while packing, since you won’t be switching rooms, and you will unpack easier.

    Labeling is important as well!

    Room-by-room packing is a great idea because it is a process that will allow you to be efficient. But, without labeling, that is almost impossible. Write the name of the room where the box belongs, on every single box. Write the list of the things that are inside as well, so if you need anything as soon as you get to your new home, you can find it with ease. Markers are the best choice for labeling moving boxes, so don’t forget those.

    Color-coding

    Now that you decided that you want to be moved by the professional movers, you need to make sure they understand that you packed room by room. And, that you will be unpacking the same way. So, color coding is your best shot at success. A great example is painting the boxes from the living room blue, and putting a blue note on the living room door. Choose the different colors on each room doors and pair the boxes. Tell your movers what the plan is as well. It’s easy to do this if everyone is on board.

    You will have no problem unpacking and being as efficient as possible if you choose room-by-room packing and color-coding. Having all the things in the right moving boxes and the movers knowing what they need to do is a guarantee that you will get relocated fast and unpacked even faster. If you have help from your friends and family, you can be sure that your overall relocation will be a great, fun, and efficiently conducted event.
