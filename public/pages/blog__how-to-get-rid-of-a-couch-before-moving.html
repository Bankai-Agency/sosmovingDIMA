How to Get Rid of a Couch Before Moving

How to Get Rid of a Couch Before Moving

Last Updated: 
Monday, December 8, 2025

Table of Contents

    That 10-year-old couch seemed worth moving until you calculated costs: $100 to move it, but replacement costs $400 at IKEA. Now you're stuck with 200 pounds of furniture nobody wants, pickup services quoting $150, and moving day approaching fast. Here's every legal way to get rid of your couch in Los Angeles, ranked from free to expensive, fastest to slowest.

    Free Couch Removal Options

    LA Sanitation Bulk Pickup (FREE, 1-2 weeks):Best option for city residents. Schedule at MyLA311.lacity.org or call 1-800-773-2489. Place couch at curb night before. They take up to 4 items. Cannot be blocked by cars.

    Wait times:

    • Regular season: 7-10 days
    • Peak moving season: 2-3 weeks
    • After holidays: 3-4 weeks

    "Free" Section Success (FREE, 0-3 days):

    Craigslist Free:Post with photos. Title: "FREE COUCH - FIRST COME - CURB ALERT"Gone in: 2-48 hours usually

    Facebook Marketplace:List as free. Reaches neighbors quickly.Success rate: 70% within 3 days

    Nextdoor:"Free couch, must go today!"Best for: Same neighborhood pickup

    Key to success: Good photos, honest description, "first come first served," location visible.

    Donation Options (Sometimes Free Pickup)

    Who actually takes couches:

    Habitat for Humanity ReStore

    • Condition: Excellent only
    • Pickup: Free for multiple items
    • Call: (323) 989-4092
    • Reality: Reject 80% of couches

    Out of the Closet

    • Condition: Good, no stains
    • Pickup: Sometimes free
    • Multiple locations in LA
    • Benefit: AIDS Healthcare Foundation

    Salvation Army

    • Pickup: Hit or miss
    • Call: (800) SA-TRUCK
    • Scheduling: 2-3 weeks out
    • Often cancel last minute

    Reality check: Most charities reject used couches. Bed bugs fear, stains, wear. Don't count on donation.

    Sell for Removal Price

    Price to move strategy:Your couch worth $500? List for $50. Buyer handles removal. You avoid disposal costs.

    Where to list:

    • Facebook Marketplace: Fastest
    • OfferUp: Local buyers
    • Craigslist: Still works
    • Nextdoor: Neighbors

    Magic words: "Priced to move today!" "Must go by [date]" "You haul"

    Success rate: 60% if priced right

    Paid Removal Services

    Junk haulers:

    • 1-800-GOT-JUNK: $129-189 (same day)
    • College Hunks: $100-150
    • LoadUp: $79-129
    • TaskRabbit: $50-100

    What's included:

    • They lift/carry
    • Same or next day
    • Stairs no problem
    • Multiple items possible

    Hidden costs: Minimum charges apply. One couch costs same as three.

    The Apartment Dumpster Dilemma

    Legal? Usually no.Risk: $500 fine, angry managementReality: People do it anyway

    If you must:

    • Break down first
    • Late night/early morning
    • Not all at once
    • Mix with regular trash

    Better option: Ask management about bulk disposal days

    DIY Disposal Routes

    Rent truck, drive to landfill:

    • U-Haul: $29.95 + mileage
    • Landfill fee: $35-50
    • Gas: $20
    • Your time: 3 hours
    • Total: $100+

    Locations accepting couches:

    • Calabasas Landfill
    • Scholl Canyon Landfill
    • Southeast Resource Recovery

    Must show LA residency.

    Breaking Down for Regular Trash

    Tools needed:

    • Reciprocating saw: $40
    • Box cutter
    • Screwdriver
    • 2 hours patience

    Process:

    1. Remove cushions (trash bag)
    2. Cut fabric (trash bag)
    3. Disassemble frame (regular trash)
    4. Cut foam (multiple bags)
    5. Spread over 2-3 weeks

    Legal but exhausting.

    Special Circumstances

    Bed bugs/infested:

    • MUST wrap completely in plastic
    • Mark "BED BUGS" clearly
    • Special pickup required
    • Costs $200-400
    • Illegal to donate/give away

    Leather couches:

    • Harder to donate (smell issues)
    • Recycling centers sometimes interested
    • Upholstery shops might want leather

    Sleeper sofas:

    • Extra heavy (contains bed frame)
    • Most expensive to remove
    • Scrap metal value inside

    Timing Your Disposal

    Start early:

    • 3 weeks before: Try selling
    • 2 weeks before: Schedule free pickup
    • 1 week before: List as free
    • 3 days before: Call junk haulers
    • Day before: Panic options

    Don't wait until moving day.

    Illegal Disposal Penalties

    Street dumping: $500-1,500 fineAlley dumping: $500 fine + cleanup costsOther's dumpster: Trespassing chargesVacant lot: Criminal prosecution possible

    LA has cameras everywhere. You will get caught.

    Couch Conditions Nobody Wants

    Automatic rejections:

    • Pet damage/smell
    • Visible stains
    • Torn fabric
    • Broken frame
    • Cigarette burns
    • Missing cushions
    • Bed bugs ever

    If yours has these, go straight to paid removal.

    The "Moving Sale" Strategy

    Three days before moving:

    • Everything must go sale
    • Couch priced at $25
    • "Free if you take today"
    • Usually works

    People buy things at moving sales they'd never buy otherwise.

    City-Specific Rules

    Beverly Hills: Free bulk pickup monthlySanta Monica: Call for appointmentBurbank: $37 pickup feeGlendale: Online scheduling requiredPasadena: Twice yearly freeLong Beach: Call (562) 570-2876

    Check your specific city website.

    Environmental Options

    Couch recycling exists:

    • Wood frame: Recyclable
    • Metal springs: Valuable
    • Foam: Sometimes recyclable
    • Fabric: Usually landfill

    DR Mattress Recycling:Also takes couches. $45-80 pickup.

    Emergency Day-Before Options

    Out of time?

    1. LoadUp app (same day, $79+)
    2. TaskRabbit (2-hour response)
    3. Craigslist "gigs" section
    4. College kids + $50 cash
    5. Include in moving costs

    Ask movers: "Extra $50 to take couch to dump?" Some agree.

    Cost-Benefit Analysis

    Cost to move couch: $50-100Cost to dispose: $0-150New couch delivered: $400+Your back health: Priceless

    Usually cheaper to dispose and buy new.

    Make the Decision

    Calculate: Moving cost + old couch value vs disposal cost + new couch price. Most discover disposal wins. That 10-year-old couch already gave you its value.
