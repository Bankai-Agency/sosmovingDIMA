Reasons to Move to Pasadena as a Senior

Last Updated: 
Friday, March 19, 2021

Table of Contents

    When you are a senior and currently living your golden age in retirement, you have the freedom to choose your own city. You do not have to worry about finding a place with a good job market, great schools, short commute time, and so on. The only thing you should worry about is finding a place where you can feel comfortable and happy. One such place would be Pasadena. This city is popular to smell roses and enjoy life. Therefore, here are all the reasons to move to Pasadena as a senior. If you happen to like this city, make sure to hire movers Los Angeles on time so they can help you with your relocation.

    Move to Pasadena as a senior because of the weather

    Most people after they reach the age of 70 react differently to the weather. It is better for seniors to spend their time in an area where the weather is warm. This is the first reason why you should move to Pasadena after your retirement. This city has a Mediterranean climate. This means that it has long hot summers that last well until late October and somewhat cold winters. Usually, June, July, and August are the hottest with 90 degrees on average while December, January, and February are the coldest with 40 degrees on average. This means that you can spend most of your time outside, taking a long walk, socializing with other people, and enjoying your life. Since the temperatures are pretty high, make sure that moving companies Pasadena take care of your entire relocation instead of you. You need to take care of your health.

    Neighbors to choose from

    Another great thing about Pasadena would be its diverse neighborhoods. Here are some neighborhoods you should consider for your future home.

    • Bungalow Heaven – homes built between 1900 and 1930
    • Daisy Villa – has the most parks in the city
    • Lexington Heights – usually popular among families with young children
    • Prospect Park – another quiet residential neighborhood

    Since you need to buy a house, it would be smart to hire a real estate agent. This is especially beneficial for you if you do not know Pasadena. When you contact a real state agency, you should tell them what type of house you want, your budget, and other things you find important. In addition to this, maybe you can ask a real estate agent to recommend you Los Angeles apartment movers if you still have not hired a moving company. Usually, real estate agencies would know reliable and professional moving companies.

    Move to Pasadena as a senior because of the living costs

    If you live in a huge city, it can be quite expensive. Unfortunately, you are going to receive less money when you retire. Of course, this depends on your job position before your retirement. However, usually, it is less than your salary. You need to find a city with acceptable living costs so you can afford your life there. Pasadena is cheaper than any large city in its vicinity. It is a little bit more expensive than other smaller cities. Still, it is pretty affordable. For example, a median family income is around $76,000 while a median home price would be around $470,000. Since you will probably live only with your partner, you can save money by buying a smaller house rather than a huge family one. In addition to this, you can also see if you can buy a house with a small garden or a backyard.

    Culture & Contemporary Life

    Pasadena is everything but a boring city. You will find all sorts of entertainment options geared toward all age groups. First of all, you should start your every new year with the Tournament of Roses Parade. It is one of the most popular parades in the country filled with roses and marching bands. Then, you should visit the downtown as it is the most interesting place in the city. There are many restaurants, shops, yoga places, cultural centers, and so on in this area. In addition to this, you can also find senior groups where you can join, meet new people, find a new hobby, and something similar. It is important to find a group of people to spend time with. This is especially important when you a senior. You should still be active despite your age and engage in activities that you can handle.

    Move to Pasadena as a senior because of the food and parks

    Another important thing to consider when moving to a new city would be the food. Luckily, Pasadena has one of the best food scenes in the entire state. You can find everything from Japanese food, Indian food, Mexican food, and so on. After a good meal, it is time to be active. There are many parks around the city where you can walk around, breathe the fresh air, and spend time with other people. This is also perfect if you have a pet. As you might know, it is extremely beneficial to have a pet, especially if you live alone. For this reason, after your move, you should consider adopting a cat or a dog. If you adopt a dog, then you need to take them for a walk every single day. This is also a perfect opportunity to leave your house every day and be physically active.

    All the pros and cons

    Here are all the reasons to move to Pasadena as a senior. First of all, because of the warm weather. Then, you will get to enjoy a beautiful view to the north of the San Gabriel mountains. do not forget about great restaurants and all the cultural centers in the city. Then, you can spend time outside in some of the parks in the city. In addition to this, the city is quite close to the beach or even to the mountains. You will have a lot of choices for entertainment. However, some disadvantages to living in this city would be bad traffic, especially during rose parade event. Sometimes, air quality can be quite bad but it is durable.
