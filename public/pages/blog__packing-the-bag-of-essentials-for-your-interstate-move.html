Packing the Bag of Essentials for Your Interstate Move

Packing the Bag of Essentials for Your Interstate Move

Last Updated: 
Saturday, August 28, 2021

Table of Contents

    It’s time to move! You will certainly have a lot of obligations, decisions that need to be made, and a lot of financial expenses. In order for everything to go well, you must be well prepared and organized. Here you will find the best tips on packing the bag of essentials for your interstate move and that way you will always have everything you need by your side. One of the best moving companies like SOS Moving LA will be your right hand in the relocation but you will also play a big role in this process.

    Packing the bag of essentials for your interstate move – Easy or not?

    Yes, it’s easy! When long distance movers Los Angeles come to you, you must keep everything under control. That is why it is important to have a bag with important things when moving, always at hand. Of course, you know what you need and what you don’t, and that’s how you decide what your luggage will look like. Keep in mind that you need to pack some things to be with you so that you can react quickly in case of unforeseen circumstances.

    What can be an essential bag?

    It is your wish that you choose to be the place where you will keep your key things when moving interstate. It can usually be a box like any other where you will put all your essentials next to each other. However, as water and other liquids can often be here, a plastic box with partitions is also recommended, where you will separate everything nicely. Be sure to mark the box where you keep your essentials for the moving day so that your people from packing services Los Angeles would not be confused with the rest of your belongings.

    You can also use a smaller or larger bag, which is easier to carry on the go. The most important thing is to be careful where your essential bag is all the time, and not to lose sight of it, because that is exactly the point of packing a box or bag like this. Packing the bag of essentials for your interstate relocation is an important thing and will help you a lot on the day of moving and after.

    What needs to be inside?

    • Important Documents
    • Electronics (Charger, Laptop)
    • Medications
    • Pet items
    • Toilet Paper
    • A Change of Clothes
    • Toothbrush and Basic Toiletries
    • Snacks and Water

    Documents- second thing when packing the bag of essentials for your interstate move

    Keeping sensitive and important documents safe is important while you move interstate. Everything that is of great importance from your documents should be with you when you move interstate. These mainly include documents such as ID cards, passports, medical records, apartment contracts, and bank paperwork.

    Electronics

    There’s nothing worse than the feeling of your phone running out of battery and not being able to find your charger, especially during a big move when you’re likely coordinating with your new landlord, family members, moving company, and any others involved in the process.  Make sure to pack your phone charger, along with any other important electronics, so they don’t get lost or stolen during the move. If you are moving your business, your office movers Los Angeles will pack all your expensive equipment, so it is important that you keep something important to you.

    Medications – first thing when packing the bag of essentials for your interstate move

    Medication is very important to be a part of packing essential bag when relocating interstate. Moving can be difficult for the body and head, so it is advisable to bring some painkillers if this happens. For example, take sleeping pills with you to rest before unpacking. Also, if you already have a prescribed therapy that you take regularly, make sure it is in your bag so that you can drink it regardless of the duration of the move. Also, this is a good option. And if you do not want to entrust your medicines and medical documentation to a moving company, and you want only you to have access to them.

    Pet items

    If you’re moving interstate with pets, it’s a good idea to pack them their own essentials bag. Make sure to include a portable water dish, a couple of days of food, some toys as needed, and any medications. You might also want to check in with your vet before you move to see how you can ease the transition for your furry friend. As they could have some good recommendations for your particular pet.

    Toilet Paper

    When moving into a new space, it’s best not to assume that there will be toilet paper waiting for you when you get there. Packing that extra roll provides some peace of mind. And an unnecessary quick trip to the store when you’re in the thick of moving! When you are packing the bag of essentials things for your interstate move, this must be one of the more significant things.

    A Change of Clothes

    Clothes are often some of the first things we pack. And also some of the most daunting things to unpack when you get to a new home. In case you don’t want to rummage through your boxes when you first arrive in your new place, we recommend also packing a change of clothes. That includes a full outfit, socks, and underwear!

    Toothbrush and Basic Toiletries

    If there’s one thing that can make you feel refreshed, it’s brushing your teeth, putting on deodorant, and brushing your hair. Make sure to pack the basic essentials in your moving bag so you can feel refreshed throughout the process.

    Snacks and Water

    Moving is tiring work, both physically and emotionally. Chances are when you finally arrive and get all your items into your new space, you will be ready for a quick snack and a good night’s sleep. So make sure to pack some dry and easy-to-access snacks in your bag. And finally- hydrate! It’s so easy to forget to drink water. Having a bottle in your essentials bag when moving across the country means it will be easy to access when you remember to take a well-earned break.

    Now that you know what packing the bag of essentials for your interstate move should look like, you are ready to move of any kind. With a bag of essentials, you will be safe at all times and there will be no unforeseen situations for you. When you arrive at your new address, you will see how much this can help you to quickly. And easily overcome all the obligations that await you. Good luck!
