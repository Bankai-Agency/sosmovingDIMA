Last Updated:

    The question "how much do movers cost" in Los Angeles gets you answers ranging from $50 to $500 per hour. This massive range exists because companies quote differently, hide fees creatively, and serve different market segments. Here's what moving actually costs in LA in 2025, including every fee you'll encounter and exactly how to calculate your total.

    Minimum Hours Requirements

    Every LA mover has minimums:

    • Standard minimum: 3 hours
    • Weekend minimum: Often 4 hours
    • Holiday minimum: 4-6 hours
    • Long distance (30+ miles): 4 hours

    You need 2 hours of moving? You pay for 3. That's industry standard.

    • Total cost: $357-387

    • Better to pay slightly more for faster service

    Hidden Fees Exposed

    "Double drive time" (California law):Moves over 10 miles require charging travel time both ways. Not optional—it's CPUC regulation.

    Stair fees:Some charge $50-100 per floor. SOS Moving? Included.

    Long carry charges:Distance from truck to door over 75 feet. Some charge $50-150. SOS Moving? Included.

    Packing material markups:

    • Box elsewhere: $5-8
    • SOS Moving: $4-6
    • Difference adds up fast

    Credit card processing:3-5% typical. SOS Moving builds it into card price—no surprise fees.

    Seasonal Price Variations

    Peak season (May-September):

    • Rates increase 20-30%
    • Minimum hours increase
    • Availability decreases
    • Book 3-4 weeks ahead

    Off-season (November-February):

    • Better rates available
    • More flexibility
    • Same-week booking possible
    • Negotiate better deals

    Month-end premium:Everyone moves on the 31st/1st. Rates jump 25-40%. Move mid-month = save money.

    DIY "savings":

    • U-Haul truck: $29.95 + $1.29/mile = $150
    • Gas: $60
    • Equipment rental: $50
    • Pizza for friends: $50
    • Your time (8 hours): Priceless
    • Chiropractor later: $200
    • Total: $510 + pain

    Professional movers:

    • Complete service: $400-600
    • No injury risk
    • Insured protection
    • Done in half the time
    • Total: Peace of mind

    Ways to Reduce Moving Costs

    Pack yourself: Save $200-500 but takes 20+ hours

    Provide materials: Save $100-200 buying boxes yourself

    Move mid-week: Monday-Thursday 15% cheaper

    Move mid-month: Avoid end-month rush pricing

    Declutter first: Less stuff = fewer hours

    Be ready: Movers waiting = you paying hourly

    Book early: Last-minute costs 20-30% more
