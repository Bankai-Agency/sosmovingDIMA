Regulatory Compliance

SOS Moving & Storage is a regulated service provider operating under licensing by the California Public Utilities Commission (CPUC). We maintain full compliance with applicable federal, state, and municipal transportation and service regulations.

CPUC Compliance

All local moves within California are conducted in accordance with CPUC tariff and service requirements, including Double Drive Time regulations for moves exceeding 10 miles.

Interstate Operations

All long-distance and interstate relocations comply with Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) requirements.

Structured Service Execution Protocols

Every SOS Moving project — whether residential, commercial, or institutional — follows a defined operational process designed to ensure consistency, quality control, and regulatory compliance.

01

Step 1
Requirements Analysis
Client requirements are documented during the initial intake process, including inventory assessment, distance calculations, access conditions, and any special handling needs.

02

Step 2
Compliance Evaluation
Each project is reviewed for regulatory requirements, building access rules, COI needs, parking permits, and any client-specific or program-specific compliance standards.

03

Step 3
Cost & Pricing Modeling
Our pricing team calculates project costs based on resource requirements, distance, seasonal demand factors, and contract terms. All pricing is transparent and documented prior to service execution.

04

Step 4
Operational Planning
Resource allocation (crew size, truck type, equipment), route planning, timeline, and contingency protocols are defined before the move date.

05

Step 5
Service Execution
Trained field crews execute the move according to the operational plan, maintaining protection standards, communication protocols, and quality checkpoints throughout the process.

06

Step 6
Completion & Documentation
Post-move walkthrough, client sign-off, and documentation are completed. For institutional clients, audit-ready records are maintained per contract requirements.

Documentation & Audit-Ready Processes

For institutional and contract-based engagements, SOS Moving maintains comprehensive operational documentation including:

  • Service agreements and scope-of-work documents
  • Inventory records and condition reports
  • Certificates of Insurance (COI)
  • Crew assignment and vehicle logs
  • Completion confirmations and client sign-offs
  • Compliance checklists per project type

Insurance & Liability Coverage

SOS Moving carries comprehensive liability insurance on every move. Basic carrier liability provides coverage at $0.60 per pound per article. Full-value protection is available through our insurance partner, Baker International, for clients requiring enhanced coverage.
‍
Certificates of Insurance are provided free of charge for all projects requiring COI documentation.
