    When you want to move your entire household, it means almost every single item that you own. This can be extremely hard just by the sheer number of items that you have. However, not all items are easy to pack and move. This usually depends on their weight, shape, size, and so on. Usually, the bigger or the bulkier item is, the more difficult it is to relocate. For this reason, you should hire the best moving companies Los Angeles to see how you should approach this hard task. You have two options. You can either leave everything to your movers or you can be responsible for packing large and bulky items. If you choose to do it by yourself, here is a guide for you to follow.

    Packing large and bulky items after hiring a moving company

    First of all, you need to find a suitable movie company for your relocation. It is almost impossible to relocate your entire household across the country by yourself. For this reason, you should try to find Los Angeles interstate movers for your relocation. They are professionals after all so they will know how to do everything properly while minimizing all the damage that could occur to your items. This is especially important since you plan to move your large and bulky items. Because of their size and weight, they are easily damaged during transport. This is why you need professional help in order to prevent any possible damages. Even if you want to pack these items on your own, you can still ask your movers for a couple of pieces of advice.

    Consider getting professional packing services

    On a similar note, you should also consider getting professional packing services. As you might know, you can actually choose which services you want to use from a moving company. Sometimes, you might just want to use movers to transport your household items to your new address. Other times, you can also add packing services Los Angeles to your moving bill. If you decide that you want to use professional packing services, you will basically have much more time to do other moving-related tasks that your movers cannot take care of. However, keep in mind that if you plan to use professional packing services, it is going to be more expensive. If you are on a tight budget, then, it would be better to do everything by yourself. This is the reason why it is important to determine your moving budget before relocating.

    Packing large and bulky items with packing supplies

    You cannot pack any items without gathering packing supplies first. The good thing would be that you can use most of the common packing supplies for your large items. These would be the following.

    • Moving boxes – you can find them at any local supermarkets, offices, liquor stores, from moving companies Glendale CA, etc. make sure to get enough moving boxes since you will use them for every single item that you have in your household.
    • Bubble wrapping – protect your items.
    • Moving blanket – for the large items that you cannot disassemble.
    • Moving straps – you will need them in order to secure bubble wrapping or moving blankets.
    • A dolly – you can transport your large and bulky items to the moving truck.
    • Zip bags – where you can keep smaller parts of your items.

    Disassemble your items before packing them

    Now that you have everything ready, it is time to disassemble your large and bulky items. First of all, you will need suitable tools and equipment for this job. Then, you should find manuals or look at tutorials on YouTube. If you’re not sure what to do, you can always ask your friends and family to help you. Usually, everyone has at least one person who is good at dissembling and assembling furniture. In addition to this, it is so much more interesting to disassemble your large furniture with your friend rather than doing everything on your own. This is a good way to spend quality time with your family members or friends before you move away. Furthermore, it is almost impossible to disassemble large and bulky items on your own. You will only end up hurting yourself or damaging your items.

    Tips for packing large and bulky items

    After you disassemble your large and bulky items, it is time to pack them alone or with movers California to New York. You should put all the small bolts in other parts into zip bags. Make sure to label those bags so you will not lose any bolts when you need to reassemble your items again. Then, for larger parts that cannot fit into bags, you need to use bubble wrapping. Make sure to secure bubble wrapping with either tape or moving straps. You should put those pieces in two moving boxes. Again, make sure to label moving boxes. If you cannot disassemble your large items, then you will have to move them in one piece. Still, you will have to protect him from any potential damage. You will do this by covering them with moving blankets. Make sure to secure the blanket with moving straps.

    Transporting your items

    After you’re done with packing large and bulky items, it is time to transport them to the moving truck. As mentioned before, you need to use a dolly for this purpose. It is going to be much easier if you use a dolly instead of carrying large and bulky items in your hands. This is the part where usually your movers will step in and do it for you. Your main job is to properly pack and secure all the items so they will not sustain any damage during the transport. If you happen to do this part on your own as well, make sure to find the right place for your items in the moving truck. This means they won’t be able to move during transport.
