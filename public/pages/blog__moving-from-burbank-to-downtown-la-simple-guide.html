    Moving from Burbank to downtown LA – the cost of housing

    One thing you should know about is the cost of housing in downtown LA. It is really expensive. If you want to rent a one-bedroom apartment, it would be around $2500. Unfortunately, downtown LA is one of the most expensive neighborhoods in Los Angeles. You will notice that many young people are living with one or more roommates. Whatever you plan to do, make sure that you do not spend more than 30% of your net income on your rent. On the other hand, if you plan to buy a house, you should visit your bank and see what you can do about a mortgage. In any case, you need to save money in order to afford housing in downtown LA. For this reason, when you are organizing your relocation, you can save money by booking Los Angeles apartment movers on time.

    Commuting to your work

    Another thing you should consider would be commuting to your work. Maybe this is one of the main reasons you want to move to downtown LA. Downtown LA is known for its many job opportunities. For this reason, many people would like to live in this area to avoid chaotic traffic in Los Angeles. By now, you probably know how hard it is to commute to Los Angeles. Sometimes, you need to wait a couple of hours to reach your house. This is definitely a huge disadvantage when living far away from your workplace. Therefore, to save time, you can move to downtown LA if your job is closed. In addition to this, you should definitely hire movers downtown Los Angeles if you decide to move. Can you imagine transporting all of your items in LA? in order to avoid this unpleasant task, you should hire professional movers.

    Moving from Burbank to downtown LA – finding a job

    As mentioned before, one of the main reasons why people decide to move to downtown LA would be because of many job opportunities. However, landing a job that pays well so you can afford life in downtown LA is not exactly easy. The best way to find a job in LA would be through networking. Set up your LinkedIn page and connect with all the right people. The major employers in LA include the following.

    • Google
    • the Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL)
    • The Walt Disney Company Studios
    • Nestle USA

    Mind you, most of these are not located in downtown LA but it would be great if you can land a job in some of these companies. In addition to this, you should not give up on finding a good job. Make sure to write a good resume and present it to as many companies as you can.

    What to do in LA?

    By now, you probably know what to do in LA. However, since you will be organizing a relocation and transporting all of your items, you need to find a way to relax. Even if you find moving services California, it is still going to be stressful and difficult. For this reason, here are a couple of ways how you can relieve stress caused by your relocation. First of all, you should definitely go for a walk. Not only with you explore and get to know your new neighborhood, but you will also relieve the stress. Then, you should visit one of the many art museums in the city. Museum of Contemporary Art, the Broad, and Getty Villa are all free so you do not have to spend more money. On the other hand, Barnsdall Art Park and Hollyhock House also deserve your attention but they have an entrance fee.

    Moving from Burbank to downtown LA – other things to know

    Before organizing your relocation, you need to consider your budget. As mentioned before, it is going to be expensive to plan everything. For this reason, you need to save money. You should start a couple of months in advance if you know that you need to move by a certain date. Keep in mind that you need to afford both relocation costs plus living expenses. It can be a little bit difficult to save money, especially when living in Los Angeles but you will need to do it. Try to avoid shopping, going out, and visiting restaurants during this transitional period. Later, once you settle into your new home and find a better job, you can pick up your hobbies. In addition to this, you should declutter your household before moving with movers Burbank CA. This will save both time and money when moving.

    Should you move or not?

    As you can see, moving from Burbank to downtown LA is a huge change and a decision you should not make lightly. It is much more expensive to live in the downtown area than in Burbank. However, living in downtown LA also has many benefits. You will be closer to all the major things happening in the city. Furthermore, you can find a better-paying job much easier than in Burbank. Lastly, you can avoid commuting hours to your work. This will save you a lot of time that you can spend with your family, friend, doing your hobby, etc.
