Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a local move cost in Los Angeles?

Our local moving rates start at $119/hour (cash) for 2 movers and a truck on weekdays. Weekend rates start at $135/hour. An average one-bedroom apartment move takes 3–4 hours with 2 movers, totaling $357–$516. Call (909) 443-0004 for a free estimate.

What is included in SOS Moving’s local moving service?

Every move includes white-glove service: moving blankets, shrink wrap, packing tape, wardrobe boxes, furniture disassembly/reassembly, TV unmounting, furniture placement, and unpacking assistance. Mileage, taxes, staircases, and multiple stops are included.

How far in advance should I book my local move?

1–2 weeks in advance recommended, especially during peak season (May–September, month-end). Same-day service available for emergencies. $50 deposit applied toward total cost.

Do you charge extra for stairs, long carries, or multiple stops?

No. These are included in our hourly rate. Only specialty items (piano: $200, heavy items 300+ lbs: $150) and $30 gas fee are additional.

What is Double Drive Time?

Per CPUC regulations, moves over 10 miles require Double Drive Time — driving time counted twice. This applies to all licensed California movers.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. CPUC licensed, USDOT 3398018 registered. Full liability coverage and workers’ compensation insurance.

Can I get a same-day local move?

Yes, based on crew availability. Call (909) 443-0004 for fastest response. Crews often available same-day, especially weekdays.
